Sura 064

Verse 1

يُسَبِّحُ لِلَّهِ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ ۖ لَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ ۖ وَهُوَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ

Whatever is in the heavens and whatever is on the earth ˹constantly˺ glorifies Allah. The kingdom is His, and all praise is for Him. For He is Most Capable of everything.

Allah glorifies Himself from all that is not befitting of Him, all that is in the heavens and all that is on the earth. To Him, glory be to Him, is the absolute control over everything, and to Him is the good and beautiful praise, and He is capable of all things.

Everything in the heavens and earth glorifies Allah. He has complete control and all praise is due to Him. He is capable of all things.

Verse 2

هُوَ الَّذِي خَلَقَكُمْ فَمِنْكُمْ كَافِرٌ وَمِنْكُمْ مُؤْمِنٌ ۚ وَاللَّهُ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ بَصِيرٌ

He is the One Who created you, yet some of you are disbelievers while some are believers. And Allah is All-Seeing of what you do.

Allah is the one who created you from nothing, so some of you are deniers of His divinity, and some of you are believers in Him, acting by His law. And He, glory be to Him, is aware of your deeds, nothing of them is hidden from Him, and He will recompense you for them.

Allah created you, yet some disbelieve while others believe. Allah sees all that you do and will hold you accountable for your actions.

Verse 3

خَلَقَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ بِالْحَقِّ وَصَوَّرَكُمْ فَأَحْسَنَ صُوَرَكُمْ ۖ وَإِلَيْهِ الْمَصِيرُ

He created the heavens and the earth for a purpose. He shaped you ˹in the womb˺, perfecting your form. And to Him is the final return.

Allah created the heavens and the earth with perfect wisdom, and created you in the best form. To Him is the return on the Day of Judgment, where He will recompense everyone for their deeds.

Allah created the heavens and the earth with purpose and shaped you perfectly. You will all return to Him on the Day of Judgement.

Verse 4

يَعْلَمُ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ وَيَعْلَمُ مَا تُسِرُّونَ وَمَا تُعْلِنُونَ ۚ وَاللَّهُ عَلِيمٌ بِذَاتِ الصُّدُورِ

He knows whatever is in the heavens and the earth. And He knows whatever you conceal and whatever you reveal. For Allah knows best what is ˹hidden˺ in the heart.

He, glory be to Him, knows all that is in the heavens and the earth, and knows what you conceal - O people - among yourselves and what you reveal. And Allah is All-Knowing of what the chests conceal and what the souls hide.

Allah knows all that is in the heavens and earth. He knows both what you conceal and reveal, and what is hidden in your hearts.

Verse 5

أَلَمْ يَأْتِكُمْ نَبَأُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا مِنْ قَبْلُ فَذَاقُوا وَبَالَ أَمْرِهِمْ وَلَهُمْ عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌ

Have the stories of those who disbelieved before not reached you ˹pagans˺? They tasted the evil consequences of their doings, and they will suffer a painful punishment.

Has there not come to you - O polytheists - the news of those who disbelieved from the past nations before you, when the evil consequence of their disbelief and the evil of their deeds befell them in this world, and for them in the Hereafter is a painful, agonizing punishment?

Have you not heard about those who disbelieved before? They faced the consequences of their actions and will suffer a painful punishment.

Verse 6

ذَٰلِكَ بِأَنَّهُ كَانَتْ تَأْتِيهِمْ رُسُلُهُمْ بِالْبَيِّنَاتِ فَقَالُوا أَبَشَرٌ يَهْدُونَنَا فَكَفَرُوا وَتَوَلَّوْا ۚ وَاسْتَغْنَى اللَّهُ ۚ وَاللَّهُ غَنِيٌّ حَمِيدٌ

That was because their messengers used to come to them with clear proofs, but they said ˹mockingly˺, “How can humans be our guides?” So they persisted in disbelief and turned away. And Allah was not in need ˹of their faith˺. For Allah is Self-Sufficient, Praiseworthy.

That is what befell them in this world, and what will befall them in the Hereafter, because their messengers from Allah came to them with clear signs and evident miracles, so they said, denying: 'Are mere mortals like us going to guide us?' So they disbelieved in Allah and denied the message of His messengers, and turned away from the truth, not accepting it. And Allah is Self-Sufficient, and Allah is Rich, He has the complete and absolute richness. Praiseworthy in His words, His actions and His attributes. He does not care about them, and their straying does not harm Him at all.

When messengers came with clear proofs, they rejected the message, asking if mere mortals could guide them. Allah is Self-Sufficient and doesn't need them.

Verse 7

زَعَمَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا أَنْ لَنْ يُبْعَثُوا ۚ قُلْ بَلَىٰ وَرَبِّي لَتُبْعَثُنَّ ثُمَّ لَتُنَبَّؤُنَّ بِمَا عَمِلْتُمْ ۚ وَذَٰلِكَ عَلَى اللَّهِ يَسِيرٌ

The disbelievers claim they will not be resurrected. Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “Yes, by my Lord, you will surely be resurrected, then you will certainly be informed of what you have done. And that is easy for Allah.”

Those who disbelieved in Allah falsely claimed that they will not be brought out of their graves after death. Say to them - O Messenger: 'Yes, by my Lord, you will surely be brought out of your graves alive, then you will surely be informed of what you did in this world, and that is easy for Allah.'

The disbelievers claim they won't be resurrected, but Allah states that they will be resurrected, and they will be informed of all they have done, which is easy for Allah.

Verse 8

فَآمِنُوا بِاللَّهِ وَرَسُولِهِ وَالنُّورِ الَّذِي أَنْزَلْنَا ۚ وَاللَّهُ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ خَبِيرٌ

So believe in Allah and His Messenger and in the Light We have revealed. And Allah is All-Aware of what you do.

So believe in Allah and His Messenger - O polytheists - and be guided by the Qur'an that He revealed to His Messenger, and Allah is All-Aware of what you do, nothing of your actions and words is hidden from Him, and He will recompense you for it on the Day of Judgment.

Therefore, believe in Allah, His Messenger, and the Quran that has been revealed. Allah is aware of everything you do.

Verse 9

يَوْمَ يَجْمَعُكُمْ لِيَوْمِ الْجَمْعِ ۖ ذَٰلِكَ يَوْمُ التَّغَابُنِ ۗ وَمَنْ يُؤْمِنْ بِاللَّهِ وَيَعْمَلْ صَالِحًا يُكَفِّرْ عَنْهُ سَيِّئَاتِهِ وَيُدْخِلْهُ جَنَّاتٍ تَجْرِي مِنْ تَحْتِهَا الْأَنْهَارُ خَالِدِينَ فِيهَا أَبَدًا ۚ ذَٰلِكَ الْفَوْزُ الْعَظِيمُ

˹Consider˺ the Day He will gather you ˹all˺ for the Day of Gathering—that will be the Day of mutual loss and gain. So whoever believes in Allah and does good, He will absolve them of their sins and admit them into Gardens under which rivers flow, to stay there for ever and ever. That is the ultimate triumph.

Remember the Day of Gathering, the day on which Allah will gather the first and the last. That is the day on which loss and disparity will appear among the creation, and the believers will gain over the disbelievers and the wicked. So the people of faith will enter Paradise by the mercy of Allah, and the people of disbelief will enter Hell by the justice of Allah. And whoever believes in Allah and acts in obedience to Him, He will erase his sins and admit him to Gardens beneath which rivers flow, abiding therein forever. That eternity in Paradise is the great success, after which there is no success.

On the Day of Gathering, people will realize their losses and gains. Those who believe and do good will have their sins forgiven and enter gardens, that is the ultimate triumph.

Verse 10

وَالَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا وَكَذَّبُوا بِآيَاتِنَا أُولَٰئِكَ أَصْحَابُ النَّارِ خَالِدِينَ فِيهَا ۖ وَبِئْسَ الْمَصِيرُ

As for those who disbelieve and reject Our revelations, they will be the residents of the Fire, staying there forever. What an evil destination!

And those who denied that Allah is the true God and denied the proofs of His Lordship and the evidence of His divinity with which He sent His messengers, those are the people of the Fire, abiding therein forever, and evil is the destination to which they have gone, which is Hell.

This verse describes the fate of those who reject faith: they will abide in Hell forever, a truly terrible destination.

Verse 11

مَا أَصَابَ مِنْ مُصِيبَةٍ إِلَّا بِإِذْنِ اللَّهِ ۗ وَمَنْ يُؤْمِنْ بِاللَّهِ يَهْدِ قَلْبَهُ ۚ وَاللَّهُ بِكُلِّ شَيْءٍ عَلِيمٌ

No calamity befalls ˹anyone˺ except by Allah’s Will. And whoever has faith in Allah, He will ˹rightly˺ guide their hearts ˹through adversity˺. And Allah has ˹perfect˺ knowledge of all things.

Nothing of harm befalls anyone except by the permission of Allah, His decree, and His measure. And whoever believes in Allah, He guides his heart to submission to His command and satisfaction with His decree, and He guides him to the best of sayings, deeds, and conditions; because the origin of guidance is for the heart, and the limbs follow. And Allah is All-Knowing of everything, nothing of that is hidden from Him.

All calamities occur with Allah's permission. Those who believe in Him will find guidance and peace in their hearts through trials, and Allah knows all things.

Verse 12

وَأَطِيعُوا اللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُوا الرَّسُولَ ۚ فَإِنْ تَوَلَّيْتُمْ فَإِنَّمَا عَلَىٰ رَسُولِنَا الْبَلَاغُ الْمُبِينُ

Obey Allah and obey the Messenger! But if you turn away, then Our Messenger’s duty is only to deliver ˹the message˺ clearly.

And obey Allah - O people - and submit to Him in what He has commanded and forbidden, and obey the Messenger, peace be upon him, in what he has conveyed to you from his Lord. If you turn away from obeying Allah and His Messenger, there is no harm to our Messenger in your turning away, but rather he is obligated to convey to you what he was sent with in a clear and explicit manner.

Obey Allah and the Messenger. If you turn away, the Messenger's duty is only to clearly convey the message.

Verse 13

اللَّهُ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ ۚ وَعَلَى اللَّهِ فَلْيَتَوَكَّلِ الْمُؤْمِنُونَ

Allah—there is no god ˹worthy of worship˺ except Him. So in Allah let the believers put their trust.

Allah alone is the only one who is rightfully worshiped, and upon Allah let the believers rely in all their affairs.

Allah is the only God worthy of worship. Believers should place their trust in Him alone.

Verse 14

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِنَّ مِنْ أَزْوَاجِكُمْ وَأَوْلَادِكُمْ عَدُوًّا لَكُمْ فَاحْذَرُوهُمْ ۚ وَإِنْ تَعْفُوا وَتَصْفَحُوا وَتَغْفِرُوا فَإِنَّ اللَّهَ غَفُورٌ رَحِيمٌ

O believers! Indeed, some of your spouses and children are enemies to you, so beware of them. But if you pardon, overlook, and forgive ˹their faults˺, then Allah is truly All-Forgiving, Most Merciful.

O you who believe in Allah and His Messenger, indeed, among your wives and your children are enemies to you, who hinder you from the path of Allah and discourage you from obeying Him, so be wary of them, and do not obey them. But if you overlook their misdeeds and turn away from them, and conceal them from them, then indeed, Allah is Forgiving and Merciful, He forgives your sins, for He, glory be to Him, is Great in Forgiveness and Vast in Mercy.

Some spouses and children may be a source of trouble, so be cautious. However, if you pardon, overlook, and forgive, Allah is Forgiving and Merciful.

Verse 15

إِنَّمَا أَمْوَالُكُمْ وَأَوْلَادُكُمْ فِتْنَةٌ ۚ وَاللَّهُ عِنْدَهُ أَجْرٌ عَظِيمٌ

Your wealth and children are only a test, but Allah ˹alone˺ has a great reward.

Your wealth and your children are but a trial and a test for you. And with Allah is a great reward for those who prefer His obedience to the obedience of others, and who fulfill the right of Allah in their wealth.

Wealth and children are tests. Allah offers a great reward for those who prioritize His obedience over others.

Verse 16

فَاتَّقُوا اللَّهَ مَا اسْتَطَعْتُمْ وَاسْمَعُوا وَأَطِيعُوا وَأَنْفِقُوا خَيْرًا لِأَنْفُسِكُمْ ۗ وَمَنْ يُوقَ شُحَّ نَفْسِهِ فَأُولَٰئِكَ هُمُ الْمُفْلِحُونَ

So be mindful of Allah to the best of your ability, hear and obey, and spend in charity—that will be best for you. And whoever is saved from the selfishness of their own souls, it is they who are ˹truly˺ successful.

So strive - O believers - in the fear of Allah with all your effort and power, and listen to the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, listening with contemplation and reflection, and obey his commands and avoid his prohibitions, and spend from what Allah has provided you, it will be better for you. And whoever is saved from miserliness and withholding the surplus of wealth, then those are the successful in all good, the winners of all that is sought.

Be mindful of Allah as much as you can, listen and obey, and spend in charity. Success belongs to those who overcome their selfishness.

Verse 17

إِنْ تُقْرِضُوا اللَّهَ قَرْضًا حَسَنًا يُضَاعِفْهُ لَكُمْ وَيَغْفِرْ لَكُمْ ۚ وَاللَّهُ شَكُورٌ حَلِيمٌ

If you lend to Allah a good loan, He will multiply it for you and forgive you. For Allah is Most Appreciative, Most Forbearing.

If you spend your wealth in the cause of Allah with sincerity and good will, Allah will multiply the reward for what you have spent, and He will forgive you your sins. And Allah is Appreciative of those who spend, with a good reward for what they have spent, Clement, not hastening with punishment for whoever disobeys Him.

If you give to Allah a good loan, He will multiply it for you and forgive you. Allah is Appreciative and Forbearing.

Verse 18

عَالِمُ الْغَيْبِ وَالشَّهَادَةِ الْعَزِيزُ الْحَكِيمُ

˹He is the˺ Knower of the seen and unseen—the Almighty, All-Wise.

And He, glory be to Him, is the Knower of all that is unseen and all that is present, the Mighty, who cannot be overcome, the Wise in His words and deeds.

Allah knows all that is seen and unseen. He is the Almighty and All-Wise.
