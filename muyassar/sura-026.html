إِلَّا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ وَذَكَرُوا اللَّهَ كَثِيرًا وَانْتَصَرُوا مِنْ بَعْدِ مَا ظُلِمُوا ۗ وَسَيَعْلَمُ الَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا أَيَّ مُنْقَلَبٍ يَنْقَلِبُونَ

Except those who believe, do good, remember Allah often, and ˹poetically˺ avenge ˹the believers˺ after being wrongfully slandered. The wrongdoers will come to know what ˹evil˺ end they will meet.

Allah has excluded from the poets those poets who have been guided by faith and done righteous deeds, and have remembered Allah often. They say poetry in the Oneness of Allah - Glory be to Him - and in praise of Him, Exalted be His remembrance, and in defense of His Messenger Muhammad, peace be upon him. They speak with wisdom, admonition, and good manners, and they stand up for Islam, satirizing those who satirize it or satirize His Messenger, in response to the disbelieving poets. And those who wronged themselves through polytheism and disobedience, and wronged others by withholding their rights, or attacking them, or through false accusations will come to know what kind of a return they will return to of evil and destruction? It is an evil destination. We ask Allah for safety and well-being.

Except those who believe, do good, remember Allah, and defend the believers. These are the good poets who use their words for good. The wrongdoers will face consequences.
