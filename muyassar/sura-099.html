Sura 099

Verse 1

إِذَا زُلْزِلَتِ الْأَرْضُ زِلْزَالَهَا

When the earth is shaken ˹in˺ its ultimate quaking,

When the earth is shaken with a mighty shaking, and the earth throws out its burdens of the dead and treasures, and man asks in alarm: 'What is wrong with it?'

This verse describes the Day of Judgment when the earth will be shaken violently, signifying the end of the world.

Verse 2

وَأَخْرَجَتِ الْأَرْضُ أَثْقَالَهَا

and when the earth throws out ˹all˺ its contents,

When the earth is shaken with a mighty shaking, and the earth throws out its burdens of the dead and treasures, and man asks in alarm: 'What is wrong with it?'

On the Day of Judgment, the earth will release all that is inside it, including the dead and its buried contents.

Verse 3

وَقَالَ الْإِنْسَانُ مَا لَهَا

and humanity cries, “What is wrong with it?”—

When the earth is shaken with a mighty shaking, and the earth throws out its burdens of the dead and treasures, and man asks in alarm: 'What is wrong with it?'

People will be in shock and ask, 'What is wrong with the earth?' as they witness these events.

Verse 4

يَوْمَئِذٍ تُحَدِّثُ أَخْبَارَهَا

on that Day the earth will recount everything,

On the Day of Judgment, the earth will tell of what was done upon it, of good or evil, and that Allah, glory be to Him, commanded it to tell what was done upon it.

On the Day of Judgment, the earth will recount everything that was done upon it, revealing all deeds.

Verse 5

بِأَنَّ رَبَّكَ أَوْحَىٰ لَهَا

having been inspired by your Lord ˹to do so˺.

On the Day of Judgment, the earth will tell of what was done upon it, of good or evil, and that Allah, glory be to Him, commanded it to tell what was done upon it.

The earth is able to speak and testify because Allah has commanded and inspired it to do so.

Verse 6

يَوْمَئِذٍ يَصْدُرُ النَّاسُ أَشْتَاتًا لِيُرَوْا أَعْمَالَهُمْ

On that Day people will proceed in separate groups to be shown ˹the consequences of˺ their deeds.

On that Day, people will return from the position of reckoning in separate groups so that Allah may show them what they did of evil and good deeds, and recompense them accordingly.

On that Day, people will be separated into groups to see the consequences of their actions. They will be shown their deeds and judged accordingly.

Verse 7

فَمَنْ يَعْمَلْ مِثْقَالَ ذَرَّةٍ خَيْرًا يَرَهُ

So whoever does an atom’s weight of good will see it.

So whoever does an atom's weight of good will see its reward in the Hereafter, and whoever does an atom's weight of evil will see its punishment in the Hereafter.

Every good deed, even as small as an atom's weight, will be seen and rewarded.

Verse 8

وَمَنْ يَعْمَلْ مِثْقَالَ ذَرَّةٍ شَرًّا يَرَهُ

And whoever does an atom’s weight of evil will see it.

So whoever does an atom's weight of good will see its reward in the Hereafter, and whoever does an atom's weight of evil will see its punishment in the Hereafter.

Every evil deed, even as small as an atom's weight, will be seen and punished.
