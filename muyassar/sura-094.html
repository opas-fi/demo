Sura 094

Verse 1

أَلَمْ نَشْرَحْ لَكَ صَدْرَكَ

Have We not uplifted your heart for you ˹O Prophet˺,

Did We not expand for you, O Prophet, your breast for the laws of religion, the call to Allah, and the adoption of noble character, and We thereby relieved you of your burden that weighed down your back, and We made you -by what We have bestowed upon you of noble qualities- in an elevated and high position?

Allah reminds the Prophet that He expanded his heart for faith and noble character.

Verse 5

فَإِنَّ مَعَ الْعُسْرِ يُسْرًا

So, surely with hardship comes ease.

So do not let the harm of your enemies deter you from spreading the message, for with hardship is ease, indeed, with hardship is ease.

Allah assures that with hardship comes ease.

Verse 7

When free from worldly tasks, strive in devotion.
