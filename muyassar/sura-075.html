Sura 075

Verse 1

لَا أُقْسِمُ بِيَوْمِ الْقِيَامَةِ

I do swear by the Day of Judgment!

Allah, the Exalted, has sworn by the Day of Account and Recompense, and by the self-reproaching believing and pious soul that reproaches its owner for neglecting good deeds and committing sins, that people will be resurrected. Does this disbelieving man think that We will not be able to gather his bones after they have scattered? Yes, We will gather them, able to make his fingers or fingertips -after gathering and composing them- a complete creation, as they were before death.

Allah swears by the Day of Judgment, affirming its reality and importance.

Verse 16

Do not rush your tongue trying to memorize ˹a revelation of˺ the Quran.

It is upon Us to collect it in your heart, and then to recite it with your tongue whenever you wish. So when Our messenger Gabriel recites it to you, then listen to his recitation and be silent, then recite it as he has recited it to you, and then it is upon Us to clarify for you what is unclear to you in its meanings and rulings.

Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) was instructed not to rush while receiving revelation. Allah is responsible for ensuring the Quran is preserved in his heart.
