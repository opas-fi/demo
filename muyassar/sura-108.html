Sura 108

Verse 1

إِنَّا أَعْطَيْنَاكَ الْكَوْثَرَ

Indeed, We have granted you ˹O Prophet˺ abundant goodness.

Indeed, We have given you - O Prophet - abundant good in this world and the Hereafter, and that includes the river Al-Kawthar in Paradise, whose banks are tents of hollow pearls, and whose mud is musk.

Allah declares that He has bestowed abundant goodness upon the Prophet Muhammad, which includes the river Al-Kawthar in Paradise.

Verse 2

فَصَلِّ لِرَبِّكَ وَانْحَرْ

So pray and sacrifice to your Lord ˹alone˺.

So dedicate your entire prayer to your Lord, and sacrifice your offering for Him and in His name alone.

This verse instructs the Prophet to dedicate his prayers and sacrifices to Allah alone.

Verse 3

إِنَّ شَانِئَكَ هُوَ الْأَبْتَرُ

Only the one who hates you is truly cut off ˹from any goodness˺.

Indeed, your hater and the hater of what you have brought of guidance and light, is the one who will have no legacy, cut off from every good.

The verse states that those who hate the Prophet will be cut off from all good.
