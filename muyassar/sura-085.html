Sura 085

Verse 1

وَالسَّمَاءِ ذَاتِ الْبُرُوجِ

By the sky full of constellations,

Allah Almighty swore by the sky with its constellations through which the sun and moon pass, and by the Day of Resurrection when Allah promised to gather all of creation, and by a witness and one witnessed. Allah - Glory be to Him - swears by whatever He wills of His creation, but it is not permissible for a created being to swear by anything other than Allah, for swearing by other than Allah is polytheism. Cursed are those who dug a great trench in the earth to torture the believers, and lit a fierce fire with fuel, as they sat by the trench, clinging to it, and they were present, witnessing the torment and torture they inflicted upon the believers. And they did not take them with such severe punishment except that they were believers in Allah, the Mighty, who cannot be overcome, the Praiseworthy in His words, deeds, and attributes, who has dominion over the heavens and the earth, and He - Glory be to Him - is a witness over all things, nothing is hidden from Him.

Allah swears by the sky filled with constellations, highlighting their grandeur as a sign of His power.

The people did not learn from that. Rather, those who disbelieved are in continuous denial, as was the custom of those before them, and Allah has encompassed them with knowledge and power. This verse questions if the story of destroyed forces such as Pharaoh's has reached the Prophet (PBUH), as a warning.
