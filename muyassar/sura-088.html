Verse 1

Has there come to you - O Messenger - the news of the overwhelming Resurrection?

This verse asks if the news of the overwhelming event of Resurrection has reached the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

Verse 8

Verse 17

Do they not ever reflect on camels—how they were ˹masterfully˺ created;

Do not the disbelievers who deny the truth look at the camels: how were they created with this wondrous creation? And at the sky: how was it raised with this marvelous elevation? And at the mountains: how were they firmly set, so stability was established for the earth? And at the earth: how was it spread out and made level?

This verse prompts reflection on the creation of camels and how they were skillfully designed.
