Verse 1

الْحَاقَّةُ

The Inevitable Hour!

The Inevitable Event, the truth in which the promise and the threat will be fulfilled. What is the Inevitable Event in its nature and state? And what will make you know, [O Messenger], the reality of the Inevitable Event and picture its horror and severity for you?

This verse announces the coming of the Inevitable Hour, which is the Day of Judgment.
