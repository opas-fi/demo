Sura 104

Verse 1

وَيْلٌ لِكُلِّ هُمَزَةٍ لُمَزَةٍ

Woe to every backbiter, slanderer,

Woe and destruction to every backbiter of people, a defamer of them.

This verse condemns those who backbite and slander others, warning them of severe consequences.

Verse 2

الَّذِي جَمَعَ مَالًا وَعَدَّدَهُ

who amasses wealth ˹greedily˺ and counts it ˹repeatedly˺,

The one whose concern was to gather wealth and count it.

This verse describes those who are consumed by gathering and counting wealth, highlighting their greed and materialism.

Verse 3

يَحْسَبُ أَنَّ مَالَهُ أَخْلَدَهُ

thinking that their wealth will make them immortal!

He thinks that he has guaranteed for himself, by this wealth that he gathered, immortality in this world and escape from reckoning.

This verse exposes the delusion of those who believe that their wealth will make them immortal, highlighting their shortsightedness.

Verse 4

كَلَّا ۖ لَيُنْبَذَنَّ فِي الْحُطَمَةِ

Not at all! Such a person will certainly be tossed into the Crusher.

It is not as he thinks; he will surely be thrown into the Fire which crushes everything that is thrown into it.

This verse warns that such people will be thrown into *the Crusher*, referring to Hellfire, which will break everything that falls into it.

Verse 5

وَمَا أَدْرَاكَ مَا الْحُطَمَةُ

And what will make you realize what the Crusher is?

And what will make you know - O Messenger - what the reality of the Fire is?

This verse questions the reader about their understanding of *the Crusher*, emphasizing the severity of Hellfire.

Verse 6

نَارُ اللَّهِ الْمُوقَدَةُ

˹It is˺ Allah’s kindled Fire,

It is the fire of Allah, which is ignited, which from its intensity penetrates from the bodies to the hearts.

This verse identifies *the Crusher* as Allah's kindled fire, emphasizing the intensity and power of Hellfire.

Verse 7

الَّتِي تَطَّلِعُ عَلَى الْأَفْئِدَةِ

which rages over the hearts.

It is the fire of Allah, which is ignited, which from its intensity penetrates from the bodies to the hearts.

This verse explains that the fire of Hellfire will penetrate even the hearts, highlighting the extreme nature of the punishment.

Verse 8

إِنَّهَا عَلَيْهِمْ مُؤْصَدَةٌ

It will be sealed over them,

It is closed in upon them with lengthened chains and shackles so that they will not get out of it.

This verse reveals that Hellfire will be sealed shut upon its inhabitants, indicating their eternal confinement.

Verse 9

فِي عَمَدٍ مُمَدَّدَةٍ

˹tightly secured˺ with long braces.

It is closed in upon them with lengthened chains and shackles so that they will not get out of it.

This verse emphasizes the secure and inescapable nature of Hellfire, where the inhabitants are restrained by long braces.
