Sura 084

Verse 1

إِذَا السَّمَاءُ انْشَقَّتْ

When the sky bursts open,

When the sky is split open, and cracked by the clouds on the Day of Resurrection, and it obeys the command of its Lord in what He commanded it to split, and it is rightful for it to submit to His command. And when the earth is spread out and widened, and its mountains are crushed on that Day, and it throws out what is in its belly of the dead, and gives them up, and it submits to its Lord in what He commanded it, and it is rightful for it to submit to His command.

The sky will split open on the Day of Resurrection as commanded by Allah.

Verse 6

يَا أَيُّهَا الْإِنْسَانُ إِنَّكَ كَادِحٌ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ كَدْحًا فَمُلَاقِيهِ

O humanity! Indeed, you are labouring restlessly towards your Lord, and will ˹eventually˺ meet the consequences.

O mankind, indeed you are laboring toward Allah, and doing deeds of good or evil, then you will meet Allah on the Day of Resurrection, and He will reward you for your deeds by His grace or justice.

Every person is constantly working towards their meeting with Allah, where they will face the consequences of their deeds.

Verse 16

فَلَا أُقْسِمُ بِالشَّفَقِ

So, I do swear by the twilight!

Allah Almighty swears by the redness of the horizon at sunset, by the night and what it gathers of animals, insects, vermin, and other things, and by the moon when its light is complete, that you - O people - will go through multiple stages and different states: from a drop of semen to a clinging clot to a lump of flesh to the blowing of the soul to death to the resurrection and the rising. It is not permissible for a creation to swear by other than Allah, and if he did that, he would associate partners with Him.

Allah swears by the twilight (redness of the horizon at sunset).

Verse 20

فَمَا لَهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ

So what is the matter with them that they do not believe,

So what prevents them from believing in Allah and the Last Day after the verses have been made clear to them? And what is the matter with them that when the Quran is recited to them, they do not prostrate to Allah, nor submit to what came in it? The nature of those who disbelieve is only to deny and oppose the truth. And Allah knows best what they conceal in their hearts of stubbornness, despite knowing that what the Quran has brought is the truth, so give them tidings - O Messenger - that Allah - the Almighty - has prepared for them a painful punishment, but those who believe in Allah and His Messenger and perform what Allah has enjoined upon them, for them is a reward in the Hereafter that is neither cut off nor diminished.

Why do they not believe after these signs?
