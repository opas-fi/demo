Sura 087

Verse 1

سَبِّحِ اسْمَ رَبِّكَ الْأَعْلَى

Glorify the Name of your Lord, the Most High,

Glorify the name of your Lord, the Most High, and declare Him free of any partner or deficiency, a glorification befitting His Majesty, He who created the creatures and perfected their creation and beautified it. And He who ordained all decrees, then guided each creation to what suits it. And He who brought forth the green pasture, and then made it dry, withered, and changed.

Glorify the name of your Lord, the Most High, and declare His perfection.

Verse 6

Allah will help the Prophet remember the Quran and not forget it.

Verse 8

And We will ease you toward ease in all your affairs, including facilitating the acceptance of the burdens of the message, and making your religion easy and free of hardship.

Allah will make the path to the Message easy for the Prophet.

Verse 9

So, advise your people - O Messenger - according to what We have facilitated for you with what is revealed to you, and guide them to what is good for them. And focus your reminders on those from whom remembrance is hoped, and do not tire yourself in reminding those who are not affected by remembrance except with arrogance and aversion.

Remind people with the Quran, particularly those receptive to it.

The essence of the message contained in the Quran was also present in earlier scriptures.
