Sura 112

Verse 1

قُلْ هُوَ اللَّهُ أَحَدٌ

Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “He is Allah—One ˹and Indivisible˺;

Say, [O Muhammad], "He is Allah, the One, unique in divinity, Lordship, and names and attributes; no one shares with Him in that."

This verse declares that Allah is One, unique in His divinity, Lordship, and attributes.

Verse 2

اللَّهُ الصَّمَدُ

Allah—the Sustainer ˹needed by all˺.

Allah alone is the One sought for the fulfillment of needs and desires.

This verse states that Allah is the One whom all of creation needs for their needs and desires.

Verse 3

لَمْ يَلِدْ وَلَمْ يُولَدْ

He has never had offspring, nor was He born.

He has no son, nor father, nor spouse.

This verse affirms that Allah has no offspring, nor was He born; He is eternal and self-sufficient.

Verse 4

وَلَمْ يَكُنْ لَهُ كُفُوًا أَحَدٌ

And there is none comparable to Him.”

And there is none comparable to Him or similar to Him among His creation, neither in His names, nor in His attributes, nor in His actions; blessed and Exalted is He.

This verse asserts that there is nothing comparable to Allah in His essence, attributes, or actions.
