Sura 022

Verse 1

يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ اتَّقُوا رَبَّكُمْ ۚ إِنَّ زَلْزَلَةَ السَّاعَةِ شَيْءٌ عَظِيمٌ

O humanity! Fear your Lord, for the ˹violent˺ quaking at the Hour is surely a dreadful thing.

O people, beware of the punishment of Allah by complying with His commands and avoiding His prohibitions. Indeed, what happens at the establishment of the Hour, from horrors and severe movement of the earth, from which all its sides are split, is something great, its value cannot be estimated, nor its essence reached, and no one knows its manner except the Lord of the Worlds.

This verse urges humanity to fear Allah, warning of the dreadful quaking and horrors that will occur on the Day of Judgment.

Verse 2

يَوْمَ تَرَوْنَهَا تَذْهَلُ كُلُّ مُرْضِعَةٍ عَمَّا أَرْضَعَتْ وَتَضَعُ كُلُّ ذَاتِ حَمْلٍ حَمْلَهَا وَتَرَى النَّاسَ سُكَارَىٰ وَمَا هُمْ بِسُكَارَىٰ وَلَٰكِنَّ عَذَابَ اللَّهِ شَدِيدٌ

The Day you see it, every nursing mother will abandon what she is nursing, and every pregnant woman will deliver her burden ˹prematurely˺. And you will see people ˹as if they were˺ drunk, though they will not be drunk; but the torment of Allah is ˹terribly˺ severe.

On the Day you see the establishment of the Hour, a mother will forget her nursing infant whom she has fed from her breast, because of the distress that has befallen her, and the pregnant woman will miscarry her load from terror, and the minds of people will be absent; they are like drunkards from the severity of the horror and panic, and they are not drunk from wine, but the severity of the torment has deprived them of their minds and their perception.

On the Day of Judgment, the terror will be so immense that mothers will abandon their nursing infants, and pregnant women will miscarry, causing people to appear as if drunk due to the severity of the torment.

Verse 3

˹Still˺ there are some who dispute about Allah without knowledge, and follow every rebellious devil.

And some of the leaders of disbelief among people argue and doubt Allah's ability to resurrect; out of ignorance of the reality of this ability, and following the leaders of misguidance from every devil who rebels against Allah and His messengers.

Some people argue about Allah without knowledge, following rebellious devils and misguided leaders.

Verse 4

It has been decreed for such devils that whoever takes them as a guide will be misguided and led by them into the torment of the Blaze.

Allah has decreed and ordained that this devil will mislead everyone who follows him, and will not guide him to the truth, but will lead him to the blazing torment of Hell as a reward for following him.

It is decreed that those who follow these devils will be led astray and into the torment of Hell.

Verse 5

يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ فِي رَيْبٍ مِنَ الْبَعْثِ فَإِنَّا خَلَقْنَاكُمْ مِنْ تُرَابٍ ثُمَّ مِنْ نُطْفَةٍ ثُمَّ مِنْ عَلَقَةٍ ثُمَّ مِنْ مُضْغَةٍ مُخَلَّقَةٍ وَغَيْرِ مُخَلَّقَةٍ لِنُبَيِّنَ لَكُمْ ۚ وَنُقِرُّ فِي الْأَرْحَامِ مَا نَشَاءُ إِلَىٰ أَجَلٍ مُسَمًّى ثُمَّ نُخْرِجُكُمْ طِفْلًا ثُمَّ لِتَبْلُغُوا أَشُدَّكُمْ ۖ وَمِنْكُمْ مَنْ يُتَوَفَّىٰ وَمِنْكُمْ مَنْ يُرَدُّ إِلَىٰ أَرْذَلِ الْعُمُرِ لِكَيْلَا يَعْلَمَ مِنْ بَعْدِ عِلْمٍ شَيْئًا ۚ وَتَرَى الْأَرْضَ هَامِدَةً فَإِذَا أَنْزَلْنَا عَلَيْهَا الْمَاءَ اهْتَزَّتْ وَرَبَتْ وَأَنْبَتَتْ مِنْ كُلِّ زَوْجٍ بَهِيجٍ

O humanity! If you are in doubt about the Resurrection, then ˹know that˺ We did create you from dust, then from a sperm-drop, then ˹developed you into˺ a clinging clot ˹of blood˺, then a lump of flesh—fully formed or unformed—in order to demonstrate ˹Our power˺ to you. ˹Then˺ We settle whatever ˹embryo˺ We will in the womb for an appointed term, then bring you forth as infants, so that you may reach your prime. Some of you ˹may˺ die ˹young˺, while others are left to reach the most feeble stage of life so that they may know nothing after having known much. And you see the earth lifeless, but as soon as We send down rain upon it, it begins to stir ˹to life˺ and swell, producing every type of pleasant plant.

O people, if you are in doubt that Allah gives life to the dead, then We created your father Adam from dust, then his offspring reproduced from a drop of sperm, which is the semen that a man throws into a woman's womb, then by the power of Allah it turns into a clot, which is the thick red blood, then into a lump of flesh, which is a small piece of flesh the size of what can be chewed, then it is sometimes formed, meaning fully formed, ending in the exit of the fetus alive, and sometimes not fully formed, so it falls prematurely; so that we may make clear to you the perfection of Our power by directing the stages of creation, and We keep in the wombs what We will, which is the formed one until the time of its birth, and the stages are completed by the birth of fetuses as small children who grow until they reach maturity, which is the time of youth and strength and the completion of the mind, and some children may die before that, and some grow up until they reach old age and weakness of mind; so this long-lived person does not know anything of what he used to know before that. And you see the earth dry and dead with no vegetation on it, but when We send down water upon it, it moves with vegetation, opening up, and it rises and increases from its watering, and it grows from every kind of beautiful plant that pleases the onlookers.

This verse addresses doubts about the Resurrection by describing the stages of human creation from dust to a fully formed being. It highlights Allah's power through the development of the embryo, growth to maturity, and the revival of dead land through rain as signs of His ability to resurrect the dead.

Verse 6

ذَٰلِكَ بِأَنَّ اللَّهَ هُوَ الْحَقُّ وَأَنَّهُ يُحْيِي الْمَوْتَىٰ وَأَنَّهُ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ

That is because Allah ˹alone˺ is the Truth, He ˹alone˺ gives life to the dead, and He ˹alone˺ is Most Capable of everything.

That which has been mentioned of the signs of the power of Allah Almighty is a conclusive indication that Allah, glory be to Him, is the Lord who is rightfully worshipped, to whom alone worship is due. And He is the one who gives life to the dead, and He is capable of all things.

This verse highlights that Allah is the ultimate Truth, the One who gives life and has power over all things, emphasizing His right to be worshipped.

Verse 7

وَأَنَّ السَّاعَةَ آتِيَةٌ لَا رَيْبَ فِيهَا وَأَنَّ اللَّهَ يَبْعَثُ مَنْ فِي الْقُبُورِ

And certainly the Hour is coming, there is no doubt about it. And Allah will surely resurrect those in the graves.

And that the Hour of Resurrection is coming, there is no doubt in that, and that Allah will resurrect the dead from their graves for their reckoning and recompense.

The verse confirms the coming of the Hour (Day of Judgment) and the resurrection of the dead, a reminder of accountability for one's actions.

Verse 8

˹Still˺ there are some who dispute about Allah without knowledge, guidance, or an enlightening scripture,

And that argument is without knowledge, nor clarification, nor a book from Allah containing a clear proof and evidence, twisting his neck in arrogance, turning away from the truth, to prevent others from entering the religion of Allah. So he will face disgrace in this world with his defeat and exposure, and we will burn him in the Fire on the Day of Resurrection.

It describes those who argue about Allah without knowledge or guidance, highlighting the dangers of baseless arguments against faith.

Verse 11

If he lives in health and ease, he continues in his worship, and if he is afflicted with a calamity and hardship, he attributes the ill omen of that to his religion, and turns back from it, like one who turns on his face after being upright. He has lost this world because his disbelief does not change what was destined for him in his worldly life, and he has lost the Hereafter by entering the Fire. And that is a clear and evident loss. That loser worships besides Allah what can neither harm him if he leaves it, nor benefit him if he worships it. That is the straying far from the truth. He calls upon one whose harm is more certain than his benefit. How evil an ally and how evil a companion is that worshipped one.

The verse describes those with weak faith, who waver when faced with difficulties, highlighting that such wavering leads to a clear loss in this world and the next.

This verse challenges those who think Allah won't support the Prophet, stating that their rage is futile, as Allah will surely support His messenger.
