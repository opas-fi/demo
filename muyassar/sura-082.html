Sura 082

Verse 1

إِذَا السَّمَاءُ انْفَطَرَتْ

When the sky splits open,

When the sky is split open, and its order is disrupted, and when the stars fall, and when the seas are made to explode by Allah, some into others, so that their water is gone, and when the graves are overturned by the resurrection of those within them, then every soul will know all its deeds, what it has sent forward and what it has left behind, and will be recompensed for it.

This verse describes the Day of Judgment, beginning with the sky splitting open, signaling the end of the world as we know it.

Verse 6

يَا أَيُّهَا الْإِنْسَانُ مَا غَرَّكَ بِرَبِّكَ الْكَرِيمِ

O humanity! What has emboldened you against your Lord, the Most Generous,

O mankind, who denies the resurrection, what has deluded you concerning your Lord, the Generous, the Bestower of much good, the One worthy of gratitude and obedience? Is it not He who created you, then fashioned you and proportioned you, and composed you for the performance of your functions? In whatever form He willed, He formed you.

The Quran questions what makes humans defiant against their Generous Lord.

But no! In fact, you deny the ˹final˺ Judgment,

The matter is not as you claim, that you are justified in your worship of other than Allah, rather you deny the Day of Account and Recompense. And indeed, there are guardian angels over you, noble and honored with Allah, recording what they are charged with keeping count of; nothing of your deeds or secrets escapes them.
