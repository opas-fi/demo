Verse 17

قُتِلَ الْإِنْسَانُ مَا أَكْفَرَهُ

Condemned are ˹disbelieving˺ humans! How ungrateful they are ˹to Allah˺!

May the disbelieving human be cursed and punished! How great is his disbelief in his Lord!! Has he not seen from what Allah created him the first time? Allah created him from a small amount of fluid - which is sperm - then He determined its stages, then He showed him the path of good and evil, then He caused him to die and made a place for him to be buried, then if He wills, glory be to Him, He will bring him back to life and resurrect him after his death for reckoning and recompense. The matter is not as the disbeliever says and does, for he has not fulfilled what Allah commanded him of faith and acting in obedience to Him.

This verse condemns the ungratefulness of disbelievers, questioning their denial of Allah's blessings.
