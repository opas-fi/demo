Sura 114

Verse 1

قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ النَّاسِ

Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “I seek refuge in the Lord of humankind,

Say, [O Muhammad], "I seek refuge with the Lord of mankind, the One capable alone of repelling the evil of the whisperer."

This verse begins a prayer seeking refuge in the Lord of humankind.

Verse 2

مَلِكِ النَّاسِ

the Master of humankind,

The King of mankind, the One in charge of all their affairs, independent of them.

This verse declares that Allah is the Master of humankind, controlling all their affairs.

Verse 3

إِلَٰهِ النَّاسِ

the God of humankind,

The God of mankind, the One who alone is worthy of worship.

This verse affirms that Allah is the God of humankind, the only One worthy of worship.

Verse 4

مِنْ شَرِّ الْوَسْوَاسِ الْخَنَّاسِ

from the evil of the lurking whisperer—

From the harm of the devil who whispers at times of heedlessness, and disappears at the remembrance of Allah.

This verse seeks refuge from the evil of the whispering devil, who tempts and leads people astray.

Verse 5

الَّذِي يُوَسْوِسُ فِي صُدُورِ النَّاسِ

who whispers into the hearts of humankind—

The one who spreads evil and doubts in the hearts of people.

This verse specifies that the whispering devil influences the hearts of people with evil thoughts.

Verse 6

مِنَ الْجِنَّةِ وَالنَّاسِ

from among jinn and humankind.”

From jinn and human devils.

This verse acknowledges that devils can be from both jinn and humankind.
