Sura 006

Verse 1

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي خَلَقَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ وَجَعَلَ الظُّلُمَاتِ وَالنُّورَ ۖ ثُمَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا بِرَبِّهِمْ يَعْدِلُونَ

All praise is for Allah Who created the heavens and the earth and made darkness and light. Yet the disbelievers set up equals to their Lord ˹in worship˺.

Praise be to Allah for His attributes, all of which are perfect descriptions, and for His apparent and hidden blessings, both religious and worldly, He who created the heavens and the earth and what is in them, and created darkness and light, through the alternation of night and day. And in this is a sign of the greatness of Allah Almighty, and His exclusive right to be worshipped, so it is not permissible for anyone to associate anything with Him. Despite this clarity, the disbelievers equate others with Allah and associate partners with Him.

This verse emphasizes that all praise belongs to Allah, who created the heavens, the earth, darkness, and light. Despite these clear signs of Allah's power, disbelievers still associate partners with Him.

Verse 2

هُوَ الَّذِي خَلَقَكُمْ مِنْ طِينٍ ثُمَّ قَضَىٰ أَجَلًا ۖ وَأَجَلٌ مُسَمًّى عِنْدَهُ ۖ ثُمَّ أَنْتُمْ تَمْتَرُونَ

He is the One Who created you from clay, then appointed a term ˹for your death˺ and another known only to Him ˹for your resurrection˺—yet you continue to doubt!

He is the One who created your father Adam from clay, and you are his descendants. Then He decreed the duration of your stay in this worldly life, and He decreed another fixed term that no one knows except Him, may He be glorified and exalted, and it is the Day of Resurrection. Then after this you doubt Allah's ability to resurrect after death.

Allah created humans from clay and set two terms for them: one for their life on Earth and another, known only to Him, for the Day of Resurrection. Despite this, people still doubt the reality of resurrection.

Verse 3

وَهُوَ اللَّهُ فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَفِي الْأَرْضِ ۖ يَعْلَمُ سِرَّكُمْ وَجَهْرَكُمْ وَيَعْلَمُ مَا تَكْسِبُونَ

He is the Only True God in the heavens and the earth. He knows whatever you conceal and whatever you reveal, and knows whatever you do.

And Allah, may He be glorified, is the God who is worshipped in the heavens and the earth. And among the signs of His divinity is that He knows all that you conceal - O people - and what you reveal, and He knows all your deeds, good or evil; and for this reason, He - may He be glorified and exalted - alone is the God worthy of worship.

Allah is the only true God, worshipped in the heavens and earth. He knows all that is hidden and revealed, as well as all deeds. This knowledge confirms His sole right to be worshipped.

Verse 4

وَمَا تَأْتِيهِمْ مِنْ آيَةٍ مِنْ آيَاتِ رَبِّهِمْ إِلَّا كَانُوا عَنْهَا مُعْرِضِينَ

Whenever a sign comes to them from their Lord, they turn away from it.

These disbelievers who associate others with Allah Almighty, clear proofs and clear indications of the Oneness of Allah - may He be glorified - and the truthfulness of Muhammad, peace and blessings be upon him, in his prophethood and what he brought have come to them, but as soon as they came to them, they turned away from accepting them, and did not believe in them.

Despite the clear signs from their Lord, the disbelievers turn away from them, refusing to accept the truth of Allah's Oneness and the Prophet's message.

Verse 5

فَقَدْ كَذَّبُوا بِالْحَقِّ لَمَّا جَاءَهُمْ ۖ فَسَوْفَ يَأْتِيهِمْ أَنْبَاءُ مَا كَانُوا بِهِ يَسْتَهْزِئُونَ

They have indeed rejected the truth when it came to them, so they will soon face the consequences of their ridicule.

These disbelievers have denied the truth that Muhammad, peace and blessings be upon him, brought to them and mocked his call; out of their ignorance of Allah and their delusion in His giving them respite. So they will see that what they mocked is the truth and the truth. Allah will make clear the falsehood and fabrication of the deniers and will punish them for it.

The disbelievers have rejected the truth brought by the Prophet and will soon face the consequences of their mockery and denial of Allah's message.

Verse 6

أَلَمْ يَرَوْا كَمْ أَهْلَكْنَا مِنْ قَبْلِهِمْ مِنْ قَرْنٍ مَكَّنَّاهُمْ فِي الْأَرْضِ مَا لَمْ نُمَكِّنْ لَكُمْ وَأَرْسَلْنَا السَّمَاءَ عَلَيْهِمْ مِدْرَارًا وَجَعَلْنَا الْأَنْهَارَ تَجْرِي مِنْ تَحْتِهِمْ فَأَهْلَكْنَاهُمْ بِذُنُوبِهِمْ وَأَنْشَأْنَا مِنْ بَعْدِهِمْ قَرْنًا آخَرِينَ

Have they not seen how many ˹disbelieving˺ peoples We destroyed before them? We had made them more established in the land than you. We sent down abundant rain for them and made rivers flow at their feet. Then We destroyed them for their sins and replaced them with other peoples.

Did these who deny the Oneness of Allah Almighty and His exclusive right to worship, and deny His Messenger Muhammad, peace and blessings be upon him, not know what befell the denying nations before them of destruction and devastation? We had established them in the land in a way that We did not establish you, O disbelievers, and We had blessed them with the sending down of rains and the flowing of rivers from beneath their dwellings; as a gradual progression and respite for them. So they disbelieved in Allah's blessings and denied the Messengers, so We destroyed them because of their sins, and We created after them other nations who succeeded them in the construction of the earth?

The verse reminds people of the destruction of past disbelieving nations, despite Allah's favors like rain and rivers. They were destroyed for their sins, and their fate serves as a warning.

Verse 7

وَلَوْ نَزَّلْنَا عَلَيْكَ كِتَابًا فِي قِرْطَاسٍ فَلَمَسُوهُ بِأَيْدِيهِمْ لَقَالَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا إِنْ هَٰذَا إِلَّا سِحْرٌ مُبِينٌ

Had We sent down to you ˹O Prophet˺ a revelation in writing and they were to touch it with their own hands, the disbelievers would still have said, “This is nothing but pure magic!”

And if We had sent down to you - O Messenger - a book from the sky in papers, and these polytheists had touched it with their hands, they would have said: “What you have brought - O Messenger - is clear magic.”

Even if a written revelation were sent down and touched by their hands, the disbelievers would still deny it and claim it as magic, revealing their stubborn disbelief.

Verse 8

وَقَالُوا لَوْلَا أُنْزِلَ عَلَيْهِ مَلَكٌ ۖ وَلَوْ أَنْزَلْنَا مَلَكًا لَقُضِيَ الْأَمْرُ ثُمَّ لَا يُنْظَرُونَ

They say, “Why has no ˹visible˺ angel come with him?” Had We sent down an angel, the matter would have certainly been settled ˹at once˺, and they would have never been given more time ˹to repent˺.

And these polytheists said: “Why does Allah not send down an angel from the sky to Muhammad to confirm him in what he brought of prophethood?” And if We had sent down an angel from the sky in response to their request, the matter would have been decided by their destruction, then they would not be given respite for repentance, for it had been previously known by Allah that they would not believe.

The disbelievers question why a visible angel was not sent with the Prophet. If an angel had been sent, the matter would have been decisively settled with immediate punishment for them as it was already known they would not believe.

Verse 9

وَلَوْ جَعَلْنَاهُ مَلَكًا لَجَعَلْنَاهُ رَجُلًا وَلَلَبَسْنَا عَلَيْهِمْ مَا يَلْبِسُونَ

And if We had sent an angel, We would have certainly made it ˹assume the form of˺ a man—leaving them more confused than they already are.

And if We had made the messenger sent to them an angel, since they were not convinced by Muhammad, peace and blessings be upon him, We would have made that angel in the form of a human being, so that they could hear from him and address him; for it is not possible for them to see the angel in his angelic form, and if the angel came to them in the form of a man, the matter would have become confusing to them, just as the matter of Muhammad, peace and blessings be upon him, became confusing to them.

If Allah had sent an angel as a messenger, it would have appeared as a man to be understood. This would not have eliminated their confusion and disbelief, but rather increased it.

Verse 10

وَلَقَدِ اسْتُهْزِئَ بِرُسُلٍ مِنْ قَبْلِكَ فَحَاقَ بِالَّذِينَ سَخِرُوا مِنْهُمْ مَا كَانُوا بِهِ يَسْتَهْزِئُونَ

˹Other˺ messengers had already been ridiculed before you ˹O Prophet˺, but those who mocked them were overtaken by what they used to ridicule.

And since their request to send down an angel was by way of mockery of Muhammad, peace and blessings be upon him, Allah Almighty made it clear to him that mocking the messengers, peace be upon them, is not a new thing, but it had happened to the previous disbelievers with their prophets, so the punishment that they were mocking and denying would befall them surrounded them.

Messengers before you were also mocked, but the mockers were eventually punished for their disbelief.

Verse 11

قُلْ سِيرُوا فِي الْأَرْضِ ثُمَّ انْظُرُوا كَيْفَ كَانَ عَاقِبَةُ الْمُكَذِّبِينَ

Say, “Travel throughout the land and see the fate of the deniers.”

Say to them - O Messenger -: Travel through the earth and see how Allah brought about the destruction and disgrace of those who denied? So beware of a similar fate, and fear that what happened to them will befall you.

Travel the earth and observe the fate of those who denied the truth. It is a warning to the present disbelievers.

Verse 12

قُلْ لِمَنْ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ ۖ قُلْ لِلَّهِ ۚ كَتَبَ عَلَىٰ نَفْسِهِ الرَّحْمَةَ ۚ لَيَجْمَعَنَّكُمْ إِلَىٰ يَوْمِ الْقِيَامَةِ لَا رَيْبَ فِيهِ ۚ الَّذِينَ خَسِرُوا أَنْفُسَهُمْ فَهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ

Ask ˹them, O Prophet˺, “To whom belongs everything in the heavens and the earth?” Say, “To Allah!” He has taken upon Himself to be Merciful. He will certainly gather ˹all of˺ you together for the Day of Judgment—about which there is no doubt. But those who have ruined themselves will never believe.

Say - O Messenger - to these polytheists: To whom belongs the dominion of the heavens and the earth and what is in them? Say: It is to Allah, as you acknowledge and know it, so worship Him alone. Allah has prescribed mercy upon Himself, and He does not hasten to punish His servants. He will surely gather you to the Day of Resurrection, about which there is no doubt, for reckoning and recompense. Those who associate partners with Allah have destroyed themselves, so they do not believe in the Oneness of Allah, nor do they believe in His promise and threat, nor do they acknowledge the prophethood of Muhammad, peace and blessings be upon him.

Everything in the heavens and earth belongs to Allah. He has promised mercy, but will gather all for the Day of Judgment. Those who associate partners with Allah will not believe.

Verse 13

وَلَهُ مَا سَكَنَ فِي اللَّيْلِ وَالنَّهَارِ ۚ وَهُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ

To Him belongs whatever exists in the day and night. And He is the All-Hearing, All-Knowing.

And to Allah belongs the dominion of everything in the heavens and the earth, whether still or moving, hidden or apparent, all of them are His servants and His creation, and under His power, control, and management, and He is the All-Hearing of the words of His servants, the Most Forbearing of their movements and their secrets.

To Allah belongs everything in the day and night. He is All-Hearing and All-Knowing, aware of all that is hidden and apparent.

Verse 14

قُلْ أَغَيْرَ اللَّهِ أَتَّخِذُ وَلِيًّا فَاطِرِ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ وَهُوَ يُطْعِمُ وَلَا يُطْعَمُ ۗ قُلْ إِنِّي أُمِرْتُ أَنْ أَكُونَ أَوَّلَ مَنْ أَسْلَمَ ۖ وَلَا تَكُونَنَّ مِنَ الْمُشْرِكِينَ

Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “Will I take any guardian other than Allah, the Originator of the heavens and the earth, Who provides for all and is not in need of provision?” Say, “I have been commanded to be the first to submit and not be one of the polytheists.”

Say - O Messenger - to these who associate others with Allah Almighty: Shall I take as a protector and helper other than Allah Almighty, while He is the Creator of the heavens and the earth and what is in them, and He is the One who provides for His creation and no one provides for Him? Say - O Messenger -: I have been commanded to be the first of this nation to submit and yield to Him in servitude, and I have been forbidden to be one of those who associate partners with Him.

The Prophet is asked if he would take any protector other than Allah, the Creator and Provider. He is commanded to be the first to submit and not be a polytheist.

Verse 15

قُلْ إِنِّي أَخَافُ إِنْ عَصَيْتُ رَبِّي عَذَابَ يَوْمٍ عَظِيمٍ

Say, “I truly fear—if I were to disobey my Lord—the torment of a tremendous Day.”

Say - O Messenger - to these who associate others with Allah: I fear that if I disobey my Lord, and go against His command, and associate others with Him in worship, that a great torment will befall me on the Day of Resurrection.

The Prophet fears the torment of a tremendous Day if he disobeys Allah's commands.

Verse 16

مَنْ يُصْرَفْ عَنْهُ يَوْمَئِذٍ فَقَدْ رَحِمَهُ ۚ وَذَٰلِكَ الْفَوْزُ الْمُبِينُ

Whoever is spared the torment of that Day will have certainly been shown Allah’s mercy. And that is the absolute triumph.

Whoever Allah turns away that severe torment from has been shown His mercy, and that turning away is the clear victory in being saved from the great torment.

Whoever is spared the torment of that Day receives Allah's mercy, and this is the greatest victory.

Verse 17

وَإِنْ يَمْسَسْكَ اللَّهُ بِضُرٍّ فَلَا كَاشِفَ لَهُ إِلَّا هُوَ ۖ وَإِنْ يَمْسَسْكَ بِخَيْرٍ فَهُوَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ

If Allah touches you with harm, none can undo it except Him. And if He touches you with a blessing, He is Most Capable of everything.

And if Allah Almighty afflicts you - O human being - with something that harms you, such as poverty or illness, then there is no one to remove it except Him, and if He afflicts you with good, such as wealth and health, then there is no one to repel His favor or prevent His decree, for He - Glory be to Him - is the All-Powerful over everything.

If Allah afflicts you with harm, no one can remove it except Him. If He blesses you, He is capable of all things. All power is with Him.

Verse 18

وَهُوَ الْقَاهِرُ فَوْقَ عِبَادِهِ ۚ وَهُوَ الْحَكِيمُ الْخَبِيرُ

He reigns supreme over His creation. And He is the All-Wise, All-Aware.

And Allah, Glory be to Him, is the All-Dominant, the All-Subduing, above His servants; necks are bowed to Him, and the tyrants are humbled before Him, and He is the All-Wise who puts things in their proper places according to His wisdom, the All-Aware from whom nothing is hidden. And whoever is characterized by these attributes should not have partners associated with Him. And in this verse is proof of the transcendence of Allah - Almighty - above all His creation, a perfect transcendence befitting His Majesty, Glory be to Him.

Allah reigns supreme over His creation. He is All-Wise and All-Aware, and nothing is hidden from Him. This emphasizes His absolute power and knowledge.

Verse 19

قُلْ أَيُّ شَيْءٍ أَكْبَرُ شَهَادَةً ۖ قُلِ اللَّهُ ۖ شَهِيدٌ بَيْنِي وَبَيْنَكُمْ ۚ وَأُوحِيَ إِلَيَّ هَٰذَا الْقُرْآنُ لِأُنْذِرَكُمْ بِهِ وَمَنْ بَلَغَ ۚ أَئِنَّكُمْ لَتَشْهَدُونَ أَنَّ مَعَ اللَّهِ آلِهَةً أُخْرَىٰ ۚ قُلْ لَا أَشْهَدُ ۚ قُلْ إِنَّمَا هُوَ إِلَٰهٌ وَاحِدٌ وَإِنَّنِي بَرِيءٌ مِمَّا تُشْرِكُونَ

Ask ˹them, O Prophet˺, “Who is the best witness?” Say, “Allah is! He is a Witness between me and you. And this Quran has been revealed to me so that, with it, I may warn you and whoever it reaches. Do you ˹pagans˺ testify that there are other gods besides Allah?” ˹Then˺ say, “I will never testify ˹to this˺!” ˹And˺ say, “There is only One God. And I totally reject whatever ˹idols˺ you associate with Him.”

Say - O Messenger to these polytheists -: What thing is greatest in testimony to prove my truthfulness in what I informed you that I am the Messenger of Allah? Say: Allah is a witness between me and you, meaning: He is the Knower of what I have brought you and what you are going to say to me, and Allah revealed this Quran to me in order to warn you of His punishment that will befall you, and to warn with it whoever it reaches from the nations. Indeed, you acknowledge that there are other deities with Allah that you associate with Him. Say to them - O Messenger -: I do not testify to what you have acknowledged. Indeed, Allah is one God, with no partner, and I am free from every partner you worship with Him.

This verse emphasizes Allah as the ultimate witness to the Prophet's truthfulness. The Quran is revealed as a warning to all who receive it, and it firmly rejects the idea of other gods besides Allah, highlighting the concept of *Tawheed* (Oneness of God).

Verse 20

الَّذِينَ آتَيْنَاهُمُ الْكِتَابَ يَعْرِفُونَهُ كَمَا يَعْرِفُونَ أَبْنَاءَهُمُ ۘ الَّذِينَ خَسِرُوا أَنْفُسَهُمْ فَهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ

Those to whom We gave the Scripture recognize him ˹to be a true prophet˺ as they recognize their own children. Those who have ruined themselves will never believe.

Those to whom We have given the Torah and the Gospel recognize Muhammad, peace be upon him, by his descriptions written with them as they recognize their own sons. Just as their sons are not confused with others in front of them, so too Muhammad, peace be upon him, is not confused with others due to the precision of his description in their books. But they followed their desires, so they lost themselves when they disbelieved in Muhammad, peace be upon him, and in what he brought.

People of previous scriptures, like the Torah and the Gospel, recognize Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) just like they recognize their own children. However, some disbelieve due to their own desires, not lack of knowledge.

Verse 21

وَمَنْ أَظْلَمُ مِمَّنِ افْتَرَىٰ عَلَى اللَّهِ كَذِبًا أَوْ كَذَّبَ بِآيَاتِهِ ۗ إِنَّهُ لَا يُفْلِحُ الظَّالِمُونَ

Who does more wrong than those who fabricate lies against Allah or deny His signs? Indeed, the wrongdoers will never succeed.

No one is more unjust than one who fabricates falsehood about Allah Almighty, claiming that He has partners in worship, or claiming that He has a child or a spouse, or denying His proofs and evidence that He has supported His messengers with, peace be upon them. Indeed, the wrongdoers who fabricate falsehood about Allah will not succeed, nor will they achieve their demands in this world or in the Hereafter.

This verse condemns those who fabricate lies against Allah or deny His signs, stating that these wrongdoers will never succeed in this life or the next. It highlights the severe consequences of disbelief and fabrication against the divine.

Verse 22

وَيَوْمَ نَحْشُرُهُمْ جَمِيعًا ثُمَّ نَقُولُ لِلَّذِينَ أَشْرَكُوا أَيْنَ شُرَكَاؤُكُمُ الَّذِينَ كُنْتُمْ تَزْعُمُونَ

˹Consider˺ the Day We will gather them all together then ask those who associated others ˹with Allah in worship˺, “Where are those gods you used to claim?”

And let these polytheists who deny the verses of Allah Almighty beware of the Day when We gather them, then say to them: Where are your gods that you used to claim were partners with Allah to intercede for you?

This verse describes the Day of Judgment, where those who associated partners with Allah will be questioned about their false deities. It serves as a stark reminder of accountability on that Day.

Verse 23

ثُمَّ لَمْ تَكُنْ فِتْنَتُهُمْ إِلَّا أَنْ قَالُوا وَاللَّهِ رَبِّنَا مَا كُنَّا مُشْرِكِينَ

Their only argument will be: “By Allah, our Lord! We were never polytheists.”

Then their only response when they are tested and questioned about their partners will be to disavow them, and swear by Allah, their Lord, that they were not associating partners with Allah besides Him.

On the Day of Judgment, polytheists will deny their actions and falsely swear that they never associated partners with Allah. This denial emphasizes their lack of truthfulness.

Verse 24

انْظُرْ كَيْفَ كَذَبُوا عَلَىٰ أَنْفُسِهِمْ ۚ وَضَلَّ عَنْهُمْ مَا كَانُوا يَفْتَرُونَ

See how they will lie about themselves and how those ˹gods˺ they fabricated will fail them!

Reflect - O Messenger - how these polytheists lied to themselves while in the Hereafter they have disavowed polytheism? And what they used to think of their gods' intercession has vanished and disappeared from them.

This verse reflects on the lies that polytheists will tell themselves and how their false deities will utterly fail them on Judgment Day, showing the emptiness of their idolatry.

Verse 25

وَمِنْهُمْ مَنْ يَسْتَمِعُ إِلَيْكَ ۖ وَجَعَلْنَا عَلَىٰ قُلُوبِهِمْ أَكِنَّةً أَنْ يَفْقَهُوهُ وَفِي آذَانِهِمْ وَقْرًا ۚ وَإِنْ يَرَوْا كُلَّ آيَةٍ لَا يُؤْمِنُوا بِهَا ۚ حَتَّىٰ إِذَا جَاءُوكَ يُجَادِلُونَكَ يَقُولُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا إِنْ هَٰذَا إِلَّا أَسَاطِيرُ الْأَوَّلِينَ

There are some of them who ˹pretend to˺ listen to your recitation ˹of the Quran˺, but We have cast veils over their hearts—leaving them unable to comprehend it—and deafness in their ears. Even if they were to see every sign, they still would not believe in them. The disbelievers would ˹even˺ come to argue with you, saying, “This ˹Quran˺ is nothing but ancient fables!”

And among these polytheists are those who listen to the Quran from you - O Messenger - but it does not reach their hearts; because, due to their following their desires, We have placed coverings over their hearts so they do not understand the Quran, and We have placed heaviness and deafness in their ears so they do not hear or comprehend anything. And even if they see many signs indicating the truthfulness of Muhammad, peace be upon him, they will not believe in them, until when they come to you - O Messenger - after witnessing the signs indicating your truthfulness, they will argue with you: Those who denied the verses of Allah say: This that we hear is nothing but tales passed down from the ancients, which have no reality.

Verse 26

Verse 27

وَلَوْ تَرَىٰ إِذْ وُقِفُوا عَلَى النَّارِ فَقَالُوا يَا لَيْتَنَا نُرَدُّ وَلَا نُكَذِّبَ بِآيَاتِ رَبِّنَا وَنَكُونَ مِنَ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ

Verse 28

بَلْ بَدَا لَهُمْ مَا كَانُوا يُخْفُونَ مِنْ قَبْلُ ۖ وَلَوْ رُدُّوا لَعَادُوا لِمَا نُهُوا عَنْهُ وَإِنَّهُمْ لَكَاذِبُونَ

This verse highlights that disbelievers hide the truth but it will be revealed to them on the Day of Judgement. Even if they were given a chance to return, they would revert to disbelief.

Verse 29

وَقَالُوا إِنْ هِيَ إِلَّا حَيَاتُنَا الدُّنْيَا وَمَا نَحْنُ بِمَبْعُوثِينَ

They insisted, “There is nothing beyond this worldly life and we will never be resurrected.”

The disbelievers deny the afterlife, believing that this worldly life is the only existence and that there will be no resurrection.

Verse 30

وَلَوْ تَرَىٰ إِذْ وُقِفُوا عَلَىٰ رَبِّهِمْ ۚ قَالَ أَلَيْسَ هَٰذَا بِالْحَقِّ ۚ قَالُوا بَلَىٰ وَرَبِّنَا ۚ قَالَ فَذُوقُوا الْعَذَابَ بِمَا كُنْتُمْ تَكْفُرُونَ

And if you could see - O Messenger - those who deny the resurrection when they are detained before Allah Almighty for His judgment on them on the Day of Resurrection, you would see the worst state, as Allah, may He be glorified and exalted, says: 'Is this not the truth?' that is: 'Is this resurrection, which you used to deny in the world, not the truth?' They will say: 'Yes, by our Lord, it is indeed the truth.' Allah Almighty will say: 'Then taste the punishment for what you used to disbelieve,' that is: 'The punishment that you used to deny in the world because of your denial of Allah Almighty and His Messenger Muhammad, peace and blessings be upon him.'

This verse describes the disbelievers being confronted by God on the Day of Judgement, admitting to the truth of the afterlife after denying it in their earthly life. They will be punished for their disbelief.

Verse 31

قَدْ خَسِرَ الَّذِينَ كَذَّبُوا بِلِقَاءِ اللَّهِ ۖ حَتَّىٰ إِذَا جَاءَتْهُمُ السَّاعَةُ بَغْتَةً قَالُوا يَا حَسْرَتَنَا عَلَىٰ مَا فَرَّطْنَا فِيهَا وَهُمْ يَحْمِلُونَ أَوْزَارَهُمْ عَلَىٰ ظُهُورِهِمْ ۚ أَلَا سَاءَ مَا يَزِرُونَ

Losers indeed are those who deny the meeting with Allah until the Hour takes them by surprise, then they will cry, “Woe to us for having ignored this!” They will bear ˹the burden of˺ their sins on their backs. Evil indeed is their burden!

The disbelievers, who denied the resurrection after death, have surely lost. Until, when the Day of Resurrection occurs, and they are surprised by the evil fate, they will call out against themselves in regret for what they have wasted in their worldly lives, and they will be carrying their sins on their backs. How terrible are the heavy and evil loads that they carry!!

Those who deny the meeting with God will be in a state of loss and regret when the Day of Judgement arrives unexpectedly, bearing the burden of their sins.

Verse 32

وَمَا الْحَيَاةُ الدُّنْيَا إِلَّا لَعِبٌ وَلَهْوٌ ۖ وَلَلدَّارُ الْآخِرَةُ خَيْرٌ لِلَّذِينَ يَتَّقُونَ ۗ أَفَلَا تَعْقِلُونَ

Will you not understand - O polytheists who are deceived by the adornment of the worldly life - and give precedence to what remains over what perishes?

This verse emphasizes that worldly life is temporary and insignificant compared to the eternal life of the hereafter, which is better for the God-fearing.

Verse 33

قَدْ نَعْلَمُ إِنَّهُ لَيَحْزُنُكَ الَّذِي يَقُولُونَ ۖ فَإِنَّهُمْ لَا يُكَذِّبُونَكَ وَلَٰكِنَّ الظَّالِمِينَ بِآيَاتِ اللَّهِ يَجْحَدُونَ

God assures the Prophet (peace be upon him) that the disbelievers' rejection is not a reflection of his honesty, but rather their denial of God's signs.

Verse 34

وَلَقَدْ كُذِّبَتْ رُسُلٌ مِنْ قَبْلِكَ فَصَبَرُوا عَلَىٰ مَا كُذِّبُوا وَأُوذُوا حَتَّىٰ أَتَاهُمْ نَصْرُنَا ۚ وَلَا مُبَدِّلَ لِكَلِمَاتِ اللَّهِ ۚ وَلَقَدْ جَاءَكَ مِنْ نَبَإِ الْمُرْسَلِينَ

And indeed, the disbelievers have denied messengers before you whom Allah Almighty sent to their nations and they were harmed in His cause, so they were patient with that and continued in their call and striving until the help of Allah came to them. And there is no changing the words of Allah, which is what was revealed to His Prophet Muhammad, peace and blessings be upon him, of His promise to him of victory over those who oppose him. And indeed, there has come to you - O Messenger - news of those who were before you of the messengers, and what was achieved for them of the help of Allah, and what happened to their deniers of the punishment of Allah from them and His anger upon them, so you have in those who preceded the messengers an example and a model. This verse encourages the Prophet (peace be upon him) by mentioning that previous messengers were also rejected but were eventually aided by God. God's promises are always fulfilled.

Verse 35

And if it is great to you - O Messenger - the turning away of these polytheists and their turning away from responding to your call, then if you can take a tunnel in the earth, or an ascension by which you ascend to the sky, and then bring them a sign and proof of the correctness of your saying other than what We have brought them, then do it. And if Allah had willed, He would have gathered them upon the guidance which you are upon, and He would have enabled them to believe, but He did not will that for a wisdom He knows, glory be to Him, so do not be - O Messenger - of the ignorant who have been intensely saddened, and have become so distressed until that has led them to severe agitation.

If the Prophet (peace be upon him) finds the disbelievers turning away unbearable, he has the option to bring another sign to them. However, God is the one who guides and God has wisdom in allowing this situation to occur.

Verse 36

And Allah will bring the dead out of their graves alive, then they will return to Him on the Day of Resurrection to be given their account and their recompense.

Only those who are receptive and open to guidance will respond to the Prophet's call. The disbelievers, considered spiritually dead, will be resurrected by Allah and returned to Him for judgment.

Verse 37

The disbelievers demand a miraculous sign. Allah has the power to send such a sign, but He does so according to His wisdom, which most people do not understand.

Verse 38

There is no animal on Earth that crawls or bird that flies in the sky with its wings, except that they are communities homogenous in creation like you. We have not left anything in the Preserved Tablet except that We have recorded it, then indeed, they will be gathered to their Lord on the Day of Resurrection, so Allah will hold everyone accountable for what they did.

All living creatures on Earth and birds in the sky are communities just like humans. Nothing has been left out of Allah's record, and all will be gathered to Him on the Day of Judgment.

Verse 39

وَالَّذِينَ كَذَّبُوا بِآيَاتِنَا صُمٌّ وَبُكْمٌ فِي الظُّلُمَاتِ ۗ مَنْ يَشَإِ اللَّهُ يُضْلِلْهُ وَمَنْ يَشَأْ يَجْعَلْهُ عَلَىٰ صِرَاطٍ مُسْتَقِيمٍ

And those who deny Allah's proofs are deaf, they do not hear what benefits them; dumb, they do not speak the truth. So they are bewildered in darkness; they have not chosen the path of righteousness. Whom Allah wills to lead astray, He leads him astray, and whom He wills to guide, He places him on a straight path.

Those who reject Allah's signs are like the deaf and dumb, lost in spiritual darkness. Allah guides those who seek guidance and allows those who reject it to stray.

Verse 40

Say, O Messenger, to these polytheists: "Tell me, if Allah's punishment comes to you in this world, or the Hour comes upon you, in which you will be resurrected: Will you call upon anyone other than Allah there to remove the affliction that has befallen you, if you are truthful in your claim that your gods whom you worship besides Allah benefit or harm?"

The disbelievers are challenged to consider who they would call upon if Allah's punishment or the Day of Judgment were to come. It highlights that only Allah can provide help in such dire situations.

Verse 41

بَلْ إِيَّاهُ تَدْعُونَ فَيَكْشِفُ مَا تَدْعُونَ إِلَيْهِ إِنْ شَاءَ وَتَنْسَوْنَ مَا تُشْرِكُونَ

Rather, you will call upon your Lord, who created you - and no other - and you will seek His help, so He will relieve the great calamity that has befallen you if He wills; because He is able to do all things, and you will leave your idols, your false deities, and your patrons then.

In times of great distress, people instinctively turn to Allah alone. He is the only one who can remove afflictions and when He does, people forget the false deities they used to worship.

Verse 42

وَلَقَدْ أَرْسَلْنَا إِلَىٰ أُمَمٍ مِنْ قَبْلِكَ فَأَخَذْنَاهُمْ بِالْبَأْسَاءِ وَالضَّرَّاءِ لَعَلَّهُمْ يَتَضَرَّعُونَ

And We have sent - O Messenger - to groups of people before you Messengers calling them to Allah, the Exalted, but they denied them, so We afflicted them in their wealth with severe poverty and hardship of living, and We afflicted them in their bodies with diseases and pains; in the hope that they would humble themselves to their Lord, and submit to Him alone in worship.

Allah sent messengers before the Prophet Muhammad, and their people were tested with suffering so they would turn to Allah in humility.

Verse 43

فَلَوْلَا إِذْ جَاءَهُمْ بَأْسُنَا تَضَرَّعُوا وَلَٰكِنْ قَسَتْ قُلُوبُهُمْ وَزَيَّنَ لَهُمُ الشَّيْطَانُ مَا كَانُوا يَعْمَلُونَ

So why, when Our affliction came to these denying nations, did they not humble themselves to Us? But their hearts hardened, and Satan made attractive to them the sins they used to commit, and the polytheism they engaged in.

When suffering came, instead of humbling themselves to Allah, their hearts became hardened. Satan made their evil deeds appealing, preventing them from repenting.

Verse 44

فَلَمَّا نَسُوا مَا ذُكِّرُوا بِهِ فَتَحْنَا عَلَيْهِمْ أَبْوَابَ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ حَتَّىٰ إِذَا فَرِحُوا بِمَا أُوتُوا أَخَذْنَاهُمْ بَغْتَةً فَإِذَا هُمْ مُبْلِسُونَ

When they became oblivious to warnings, We showered them with everything they desired. But just as they became prideful of what they were given, We seized them by surprise, then they instantly fell into despair!

Then, when they abandoned acting upon the commands of Allah, turning away from them, We opened to them the gates of all things of sustenance, so We replaced their hardship with ease in living, and their afflictions with health in their bodies; as a gradual leading to destruction from Us, until when they became arrogant and were impressed with what We had given them of good and blessing, We seized them with punishment suddenly, and behold, they were in despair, cut off from every good.

When people ignore warnings, Allah may grant them worldly desires as a test. When they become arrogant, He seizes them suddenly and they are left in despair.

Verse 45

فَقُطِعَ دَابِرُ الْقَوْمِ الَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا ۚ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ

So the wrongdoers were utterly uprooted. And all praise is for Allah—Lord of all worlds.

So those people were annihilated and destroyed when they disbelieved in Allah and denied His messengers, and not one of them remained. And thanks and praise be to Allah, the Most High - the Creator of all things and their Owner - for the victory of His allies and the destruction of His enemies.

This verse describes the complete destruction of wrongdoers who rejected Allah and His messengers. It concludes with praise to Allah as the Lord of all worlds, highlighting His victory and ultimate authority.

Verse 46

قُلْ أَرَأَيْتُمْ إِنْ أَخَذَ اللَّهُ سَمْعَكُمْ وَأَبْصَارَكُمْ وَخَتَمَ عَلَىٰ قُلُوبِكُمْ مَنْ إِلَٰهٌ غَيْرُ اللَّهِ يَأْتِيكُمْ بِهِ ۗ انْظُرْ كَيْفَ نُصَرِّفُ الْآيَاتِ ثُمَّ هُمْ يَصْدِفُونَ

Say - O Messenger - to these polytheists: Tell me, if Allah took away your hearing, making you deaf, and took away your sight, making you blind, and sealed your hearts, so that you could not understand a word, what god other than Allah, Glorified and Exalted, is able to restore that to you?! Look - O Messenger - how We diversify the proofs for them, yet they still turn away from remembrance and consideration?

This verse challenges disbelievers to consider who could restore their senses or understanding if Allah took them away. It shows how Allah makes clear signs, yet they still turn away.

Verse 47

قُلْ أَرَأَيْتَكُمْ إِنْ أَتَاكُمْ عَذَابُ اللَّهِ بَغْتَةً أَوْ جَهْرَةً هَلْ يُهْلَكُ إِلَّا الْقَوْمُ الظَّالِمُونَ

Ask, “Imagine if Allah’s punishment were to overwhelm you with or without warning—who would be destroyed other than the wrongdoers?”

Say - O Messenger - to these polytheists: Tell me, if the punishment of Allah comes upon you suddenly while you are unaware of it, or openly and visibly while you are looking at it: will anyone be destroyed except the wrongdoers who have transgressed the limits, by directing worship to other than Allah Almighty and by denying His messengers?

This verse asks the disbelievers that if Allah's punishment came upon them, who would be destroyed other than those who have wronged themselves by disobeying Allah and His messengers?

Verse 48

وَمَا نُرْسِلُ الْمُرْسَلِينَ إِلَّا مُبَشِّرِينَ وَمُنْذِرِينَ ۖ فَمَنْ آمَنَ وَأَصْلَحَ فَلَا خَوْفٌ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا هُمْ يَحْزَنُونَ

We have sent messengers only as deliverers of good news and as warners. Whoever believes and does good, there will be no fear for them, nor will they grieve.

And We do not send our messengers except as bearers of glad tidings to those who obey us with lasting bliss, and warners to those who disobey with painful punishment. So whoever believes and confirms the messengers and does righteous deeds, then those do not fear when meeting their Lord, nor do they grieve for anything they missed of the fortunes of this world.

Messengers are sent as bearers of good news for believers and warners for disbelievers. Believers who do good will have no fear or grief.

Verse 49

The Quran is a warning for those who fear being gathered before their Lord, with no protector or intercessor but Him. This verse emphasizes being mindful of Allah.

Verse 52

And likewise, Allah the Exalted tests some of His servants with others through the differences in their portions of sustenance and morals. He made some rich and some poor, some strong and some weak. God tests people through differences in wealth and status. Disbelievers question why God favors the poor believers. God knows best who is truly grateful.

Verse 54

When believers come seeking guidance, greet them with peace and give them glad tidings of God's mercy. Those who sin out of ignorance then repent and do good, God will forgive them. God is forgiving and merciful.

Verse 55

God makes the truth clear through His signs, so that the path of the wicked becomes distinct.

Verse 56

Verse 57

The Prophet affirms his clear guidance from God, while disbelievers deny it. He does not control when punishment will come; it is only determined by God, who is the best judge.

If the Prophet had control over the punishment disbelievers seek to hasten, the matter would have been settled. However, God knows best the wrongdoers and when punishment will come.

Verse 59

God possesses all knowledge of the unseen. He knows everything that happens in the land and sea, even the falling of a leaf or a grain in the dark earth. Everything is recorded in a perfect record.

Allah takes your souls during sleep, like a minor death, and knows what you do during the day. He returns your souls each morning to complete your life's term, and you will ultimately return to Him to be judged.

Allah has absolute control over His creation. He sends angels to record our deeds. When death approaches, the angels take the soul without fail.

The Quran asks: Who rescues you from danger on land and sea? It is Allah whom you call upon in distress. If He saves you, you promise gratitude.

Allah has the power to send punishment from above, below, or to create divisions among people. These are signs, so you may understand.

Verse 70
