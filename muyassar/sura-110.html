Sura 110

Verse 1

إِذَا جَاءَ نَصْرُ اللَّهِ وَالْفَتْحُ

When Allah’s ˹ultimate˺ help comes and the victory ˹over Mecca is achieved˺,

When victory comes to you - O Messenger - over the disbelievers of Quraysh, and the conquest of "Mecca" is achieved for you.

This verse speaks of the ultimate help and victory from Allah, particularly the conquest of Mecca.

Verse 2

وَرَأَيْتَ النَّاسَ يَدْخُلُونَ فِي دِينِ اللَّهِ أَفْوَاجًا

and you ˹O Prophet˺ see the people embracing Allah’s Way in crowds,

And you see many people entering Islam in groups.

This verse describes the people entering Islam in large numbers, signifying widespread acceptance of the faith.

Verse 3

فَسَبِّحْ بِحَمْدِ رَبِّكَ وَاسْتَغْفِرْهُ ۚ إِنَّهُ كَانَ تَوَّابًا

then glorify the praises of your Lord and seek His forgiveness, for certainly He is ever Accepting of Repentance.

When that occurs, prepare to meet your Lord by glorifying Him with His praise and seeking His forgiveness frequently, for indeed, He is ever accepting of repentance from those who glorify Him and seek forgiveness, He accepts their repentance and has mercy on them.

This verse instructs believers to glorify Allah and seek His forgiveness when victory comes, emphasizing His acceptance of repentance.
