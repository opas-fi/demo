Sura 098

Verse 1

لَمْ يَكُنِ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا مِنْ أَهْلِ الْكِتَابِ وَالْمُشْرِكِينَ مُنْفَكِّينَ حَتَّىٰ تَأْتِيَهُمُ الْبَيِّنَةُ

The disbelievers from the People of the Book and the polytheists were not going to desist ˹from disbelief˺ until the clear proof came to them:

Those who disbelieved among the People of the Scripture and the polytheists were not to be parted [from disbelief] until there came to them clear evidence,

The disbelievers among the People of the Book and polytheists were not going to change until clear evidence came to them.

Verse 2

رَسُولٌ مِنَ اللَّهِ يَتْلُو صُحُفًا مُطَهَّرَةً

a messenger from Allah, reciting scrolls of ˹utmost˺ purity,

A messenger from Allah, reciting purified scriptures,

The clear proof is a messenger from Allah reciting pure scriptures.

Verse 3

فِيهَا كُتُبٌ قَيِّمَةٌ

containing upright commandments.

Within which are correct reports and just commands, guiding to the truth and to a straight path.

These scriptures contain correct teachings and just commandments.

Verse 4

وَمَا تَفَرَّقَ الَّذِينَ أُوتُوا الْكِتَابَ إِلَّا مِنْ بَعْدِ مَا جَاءَتْهُمُ الْبَيِّنَةُ

It was not until this clear proof came to the People of the Book that they became divided ˹about his prophethood˺—

And those who were given the Scripture did not become divided until after clear evidence came to them, after they recognized that he was the prophet who was promised in the Torah and the Gospel. They were united on the validity of his prophethood, but when he was sent, they denied it and became divided.

The People of the Book only divided after the clear proof came to them, even though they knew about it beforehand.

Verse 5

وَمَا أُمِرُوا إِلَّا لِيَعْبُدُوا اللَّهَ مُخْلِصِينَ لَهُ الدِّينَ حُنَفَاءَ وَيُقِيمُوا الصَّلَاةَ وَيُؤْتُوا الزَّكَاةَ ۚ وَذَٰلِكَ دِينُ الْقَيِّمَةِ

even though they were only commanded to worship Allah ˹alone˺ with sincere devotion to Him in all uprightness, establish prayer, and pay alms-tax. That is the upright Way.

And they were not commanded except to worship Allah, [being] sincere to Him in religion, inclining to truth, and to establish prayer and give zakah. And that is the correct religion, and that is Islam.

They were commanded to worship Allah alone with sincere devotion, to establish prayer, and pay alms-tax.

Verse 6

إِنَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا مِنْ أَهْلِ الْكِتَابِ وَالْمُشْرِكِينَ فِي نَارِ جَهَنَّمَ خَالِدِينَ فِيهَا ۚ أُولَٰئِكَ هُمْ شَرُّ الْبَرِيَّةِ

Indeed, those who disbelieve from the People of the Book and the polytheists will be in the Fire of Hell, to stay there forever. They are the worst of ˹all˺ beings.

Indeed, those who disbelieved among the People of the Scripture and the polytheists will be in the fire of Hell, abiding eternally therein. Those are the worst of creatures.

Those who disbelieve from the People of the Book and polytheists will abide in Hellfire forever; they are the worst of creatures.

Verse 7

إِنَّ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ أُولَٰئِكَ هُمْ خَيْرُ الْبَرِيَّةِ

Indeed, those who believe and do good—they are the best of ˹all˺ beings.

Indeed, those who have believed and done righteous deeds - those are the best of creatures.

Those who believe and do good deeds are the best of creatures.

Verse 8

جَزَاؤُهُمْ عِنْدَ رَبِّهِمْ جَنَّاتُ عَدْنٍ تَجْرِي مِنْ تَحْتِهَا الْأَنْهَارُ خَالِدِينَ فِيهَا أَبَدًا ۖ رَضِيَ اللَّهُ عَنْهُمْ وَرَضُوا عَنْهُ ۚ ذَٰلِكَ لِمَنْ خَشِيَ رَبَّهُ

Their reward with their Lord will be Gardens of Eternity, under which rivers flow, to stay there for ever and ever. Allah is pleased with them and they are pleased with Him. This is ˹only˺ for those in awe of their Lord.

Their reward with their Lord on the Day of Judgment is gardens of lasting residence and stability in utmost beauty, beneath whose palaces rivers flow, abiding therein forever. Allah is pleased with them, accepting their righteous deeds, and they are pleased with Him for what He has prepared for them of various honors. That is the good reward for those who fear Allah and avoid His sins.

This verse describes the reward for the righteous: eternal gardens with flowing rivers, where they will abide forever. Allah will be pleased with them, and they with Him. This is a reward for those who fear their Lord.
