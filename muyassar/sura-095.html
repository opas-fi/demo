Sura 095

Verse 1

وَالتِّينِ وَالزَّيْتُونِ

By the fig and the olive ˹of Jerusalem˺,

Allah swore by the fig and the olive, which are among the famous fruits, and He swore by the mountain "Mount Sinai" on which Allah spoke to Moses directly, and He swore by this secure city free from all fear, which is "Mecca", the landing place of Islam. We have created man in the best form, then We reduced him to the Fire if he does not obey Allah, and follow the messengers, but those who believe and do righteous deeds will have a great and continuous reward, without any reduction.

Allah swears by the fig and the olive, which are significant fruits, possibly referring to the land of Jerusalem where they grow.

Verse 7

فَمَا يُكَذِّبُكَ بَعْدُ بِالدِّينِ

Now, what makes you deny the ˹final˺ Judgment?

What makes you, O man, deny the Resurrection and the recompense when the proofs of Allah Almighty's ability to do that are clear?

This verse questions what makes people deny the Day of Judgment when Allah's power is evident.

Verse 8

أَلَيْسَ اللَّهُ بِأَحْكَمِ الْحَاكِمِينَ

Is Allah not the most just of all judges?

Is not Allah, who made this day to judge between people, the most just of judges in all that He created? Yes. Should creation be left to waste, neither commanded nor forbidden, neither rewarded nor punished? That is not right and it will not happen.

This verse emphasizes that Allah is the most just of all judges, ensuring fairness in the final judgment.
