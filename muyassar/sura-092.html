Sura 092

Verse 1

وَاللَّيْلِ إِذَا يَغْشَىٰ

By the night when it covers,

Allah, the Exalted, swore by the night when it covers the earth and what is on it with its darkness, and by the day when it is revealed from the darkness of the night by its light, and by the creation of the two genders: male and female. Indeed, your actions are different between one who works for this world and one who works for the Hereafter.

Allah swears by the night as it covers the earth, highlighting its function as a time of rest and peace.

Verse 5

فَأَمَّا مَنْ أَعْطَىٰ وَاتَّقَىٰ

As for the one who is charitable, mindful ˹of Allah˺,

As for he who gives from his wealth and fears Allah in that, and believes in "There is no god but Allah" and what it indicates, and the recompense that results from it, then We will guide him and grant him success in the ways of good and righteousness, and We will ease his affairs for him.

Those who give in charity and are mindful of Allah, will be guided towards good.
