Sura 103

Verse 1

وَالْعَصْرِ

By the ˹passage of˺ time!

Allah swore by time that the children of Adam are in ruin and loss. It is not permissible for a servant to swear by anything other than Allah, for swearing by other than Allah is polytheism.

This verse begins with an oath by time, indicating its importance as a witness to humanity's state.

Verse 2

إِنَّ الْإِنْسَانَ لَفِي خُسْرٍ

Surely humanity is in ˹grave˺ loss,

Allah swore by time that the children of Adam are in ruin and loss. It is not permissible for a servant to swear by anything other than Allah, for swearing by other than Allah is polytheism.

This verse declares that all of humanity is in a state of loss, emphasizing the importance of following the right path.

Verse 3

إِلَّا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ وَتَوَاصَوْا بِالْحَقِّ وَتَوَاصَوْا بِالصَّبْرِ

except those who have faith, do good, and urge each other to the truth, and urge each other to perseverance.

Except those who believed in Allah and did righteous deeds, and advised each other to adhere to the truth, and to act in obedience to Allah, and to be patient in that.

This verse states that those who believe, do good deeds, and encourage each other towards truth and patience are exceptions to the loss mentioned in the previous verse.
