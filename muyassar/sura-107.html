Sura 107

Verse 1

أَرَأَيْتَ الَّذِي يُكَذِّبُ بِالدِّينِ

Have you seen the one who denies the ˹final˺ Judgment?

Have you seen the state of that one who denies the Resurrection and the Recompense?

This verse questions the attitude of those who deny the Day of Judgment.

Verse 2

فَذَٰلِكَ الَّذِي يَدُعُّ الْيَتِيمَ

That is the one who repulses the orphan,

For that is the one who pushes the orphan away with violence and harshness from his right due to the hardness of his heart.

It describes a characteristic of the deniers as being harsh and dismissive towards orphans.

Verse 3

وَلَا يَحُضُّ عَلَىٰ طَعَامِ الْمِسْكِينِ

and does not encourage the feeding of the poor.

And does not urge others to feed the needy, so how can he feed them himself?

Another characteristic of deniers is that they neither feed the poor themselves nor encourage others to do so.

Verse 4

فَوَيْلٌ لِلْمُصَلِّينَ

So woe to those ˹hypocrites˺ who pray

So woe to those who pray, who are heedless of their prayer, do not perform it properly, nor do they offer it on time.

This verse warns those who are neglectful and insincere in their prayers.

Verse 5

الَّذِينَ هُمْ عَنْ صَلَاتِهِمْ سَاهُونَ

yet are unmindful of their prayers;

So woe to those who pray, who are heedless of their prayer, do not perform it properly, nor do they offer it on time.

This verse emphasizes that it's not just about praying, but about doing so attentively and sincerely.

Verse 6

الَّذِينَ هُمْ يُرَاءُونَ

those who ˹only˺ show off,

Those who make a show of good deeds for the sake of people.

It identifies those who pray only to show off to others as being among the heedless.

Verse 7

وَيَمْنَعُونَ الْمَاعُونَ

and refuse to give ˹even the simplest˺ aid.

And they withhold lending what is not harmful to lend, from utensils and other things; neither have they perfected worship of their Lord, nor have they done good to His creation.

This verse highlights another sign of hypocrisy: refusing to lend or give even simple aid to those in need.
