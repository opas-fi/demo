As if they, on the Day they see the establishment of the Hour, did not remain in this worldly life, due to the terror of the Hour, except for the time between noon and sunset, or between sunrise and midday.

On the Day they see the Hour, it will seem as if they had only stayed in the world for a short time, like an evening or a morning.

(c) 2025
