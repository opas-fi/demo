Sura 021

Verse 1

اقْتَرَبَ لِلنَّاسِ حِسَابُهُمْ وَهُمْ فِي غَفْلَةٍ مُعْرِضُونَ

˹The time of˺ people’s judgment has drawn near, yet they are heedlessly turning away.

The time for people's reckoning for what they have done is near, yet the disbelievers live heedless of this truth, turning away from this warning.

The time of judgment is approaching, but people are heedless and turning away from this reality.

Verse 2

مَا يَأْتِيهِمْ مِنْ ذِكْرٍ مِنْ رَبِّهِمْ مُحْدَثٍ إِلَّا اسْتَمَعُوهُ وَهُمْ يَلْعَبُونَ

Whatever new reminder comes to them from their Lord, they only listen to it jokingly,

No new reminder comes to them from the Quran, but they listen to it as if it were a game or a mockery.

When a new reminder from their Lord comes, they listen to it in jest and mockery.

Verse 3

لَاهِيَةً قُلُوبُهُمْ ۗ وَأَسَرُّوا النَّجْوَى الَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا هَلْ هَٰذَا إِلَّا بَشَرٌ مِثْلُكُمْ ۖ أَفَتَأْتُونَ السِّحْرَ وَأَنْتُمْ تُبْصِرُونَ

with their hearts ˹totally˺ distracted. The evildoers would converse secretly, ˹saying,˺ “Is this ˹one˺ not human like yourselves? Would you fall for ˹this˺ witchcraft, even though you can ˹clearly˺ see?”

Their hearts are heedless of the Noble Quran, preoccupied with the vanities of this world and its desires, and they do not understand what is in it. Rather, the wrongdoers among the Quraysh conspired in secret: to spread what prevents people from believing in Muhammad, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, that he is a human being like them, no different from them in anything, and that what he brought from the Quran is magic. So how can you come to him and follow him, while you see that he is a human being like you?

Their hearts are distracted from the Quran, and the wrongdoers secretly conspire, questioning the Prophet's humanity and calling his message 'magic'.

Verse 4

قَالَ رَبِّي يَعْلَمُ الْقَوْلَ فِي السَّمَاءِ وَالْأَرْضِ ۖ وَهُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ

The Prophet responded, “My Lord ˹fully˺ knows every word spoken in the heavens and the earth. For He is the All-Hearing, All-Knowing.”

The Prophet, peace and blessings of Allah be upon him, referred the matter back to his Lord, glory be to Him, and said: My Lord knows what is said in the heavens and the earth, and He knows what you conceal in your conversations. And He is the All-Hearing of your words, the All-Knowing of your conditions. And this is a threat and a warning to them.

The Prophet responds that his Lord knows all that is said in the heavens and the earth, and that He is All-Hearing and All-Knowing. This serves as a warning to the disbelievers.

Verse 5

بَلْ قَالُوا أَضْغَاثُ أَحْلَامٍ بَلِ افْتَرَاهُ بَلْ هُوَ شَاعِرٌ فَلْيَأْتِنَا بِآيَةٍ كَمَا أُرْسِلَ الْأَوَّلُونَ

Yet they say, “This ˹Quran˺ is a set of confused dreams! No, he has fabricated it! No, he must be a poet! So let him bring us a ˹tangible˺ sign like those ˹prophets˺ sent before.”

Rather, the disbelievers denied the Quran. Some said: It is a confused medley of dreams, having no reality. Others said: It is a fabrication and a lie, not a revelation. And others said: Muhammad is a poet, and what he brought is poetry. And if he wants us to believe him, let him bring us a tangible miracle, like the she-camel of Salih, and the signs of Moses and Jesus, and what the Messengers before him brought.

The disbelievers deny the Quran, calling it confused dreams, a fabrication, or poetry. They demand a tangible sign like previous prophets.

Verse 6

مَا آمَنَتْ قَبْلَهُمْ مِنْ قَرْيَةٍ أَهْلَكْنَاهَا ۖ أَفَهُمْ يُؤْمِنُونَ

Not a ˹single˺ society We destroyed before them ever believed ˹after receiving the signs˺. Will these ˹pagans˺ then believe?

No town before the disbelievers of Mecca whose people asked their messenger for miracles, and they were given, but they denied them and were destroyed. Will the disbelievers of Mecca believe if the miracles they requested were fulfilled? No, they will not believe.

Previous societies did not believe even after receiving clear signs, so will these disbelievers believe if they receive what they ask for?

Verse 7

وَمَا أَرْسَلْنَا قَبْلَكَ إِلَّا رِجَالًا نُوحِي إِلَيْهِمْ ۖ فَاسْأَلُوا أَهْلَ الذِّكْرِ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ لَا تَعْلَمُونَ

We did not send ˹messengers˺ before you ˹O Prophet˺ except mere men inspired by Us. If you ˹polytheists˺ do not know ˹this already˺, then ask those who have knowledge ˹of the Scriptures˺.

And We did not send before you, O Messenger, but men from among mankind to whom We revealed, and We did not send angels. So ask - O disbelievers of Mecca - the people of knowledge of the previous revealed Books, if you are ignorant of that.

Messengers sent before were only men inspired by God. The disbelievers are asked to inquire from those with knowledge of the scriptures if they do not know this.

Verse 8

وَمَا جَعَلْنَاهُمْ جَسَدًا لَا يَأْكُلُونَ الطَّعَامَ وَمَا كَانُوا خَالِدِينَ

We did not give those messengers ˹supernatural˺ bodies that did not need food, nor were they immortal.

And We did not make those messengers before you to be outside the nature of mankind, not needing food and drink, nor were they immortal and not dying.

The messengers were not supernatural beings who did not need food, nor were they immortal. They were humans like others.

Verse 9

ثُمَّ صَدَقْنَاهُمُ الْوَعْدَ فَأَنْجَيْنَاهُمْ وَمَنْ نَشَاءُ وَأَهْلَكْنَا الْمُسْرِفِينَ

Then We fulfilled Our promise to them, saving them along with whoever We willed and destroying the transgressors.

Then We fulfilled for the prophets and their followers what We promised them of victory and salvation, and We destroyed those who transgressed against themselves by disbelieving in their Lord.

God fulfilled His promise to the messengers and their followers, saving them and destroying the transgressors.

Verse 10

لَقَدْ أَنْزَلْنَا إِلَيْكُمْ كِتَابًا فِيهِ ذِكْرُكُمْ ۖ أَفَلَا تَعْقِلُونَ

We have surely revealed to you a Book, in which there is glory for you. Will you not then understand?

We have sent down to you this Quran, in it is your honor and glory in this world and the Hereafter if you remember it. Will you not understand with what We have favored you over others?

This Quran is a source of honor and glory. People are asked if they do not understand its value and significance.

Verse 11

وَكَمْ قَصَمْنَا مِنْ قَرْيَةٍ كَانَتْ ظَالِمَةً وَأَنْشَأْنَا بَعْدَهَا قَوْمًا آخَرِينَ

˹Imagine˺ how many societies of wrongdoers We have destroyed, raising up other people after them!

And many a town was its people unjust, because of their disbelief in what their messengers had brought them. So We destroyed them with a torment that annihilated them all, and We brought forth after them another people besides them.

This verse highlights Allah's power to destroy unjust societies and replace them with others, serving as a warning to those who reject His message.

Verse 12

فَلَمَّا أَحَسُّوا بَأْسَنَا إِذَا هُمْ مِنْهَا يَرْكُضُونَ

When the wrongdoers sensed ˹the arrival of˺ Our torment, they started to run away from their cities.

Then when these wrongdoers saw Our severe punishment descending upon them, and witnessed its signs, they began to flee from their town in haste.

When faced with divine punishment, wrongdoers attempt to flee from their cities, demonstrating their fear and desperation.

Verse 13

لَا تَرْكُضُوا وَارْجِعُوا إِلَىٰ مَا أُتْرِفْتُمْ فِيهِ وَمَسَاكِنِكُمْ لَعَلَّكُمْ تُسْأَلُونَ

˹They were told,˺ “Do not run away! Return to your luxuries and your homes, so you may be questioned ˹about your fate˺.”

And they were called out in this state: "Do not flee, but return to your pleasures and your enjoyment in your distracting world and your well-built dwellings, so that you may be questioned about something from your world," and this was in the manner of mockery and scorn of them.

The wrongdoers are mocked by being told to return to their former lives of luxury so they can be questioned, emphasizing the futility of their past actions.

Verse 14

قَالُوا يَا وَيْلَنَا إِنَّا كُنَّا ظَالِمِينَ

They cried, “Woe to us! We have surely been wrongdoers.”

Then they had no response except to acknowledge their crime and say: "O our destruction! We have indeed been wrongdoers by our disbelief."

The wrongdoers finally admit their wrongdoing and acknowledge their guilt, but only after they are confronted with punishment.

Verse 15

فَمَا زَالَتْ تِلْكَ دَعْوَاهُمْ حَتَّىٰ جَعَلْنَاهُمْ حَصِيدًا خَامِدِينَ

They kept repeating their cry until We mowed them down, ˹leaving them˺ lifeless.

And that saying - which was the invocation of destruction upon themselves and the acknowledgment of injustice - did not cease to be their invocation which they repeated, until We made them like harvested crops, extinguished, with no life in them. So beware - O those addressed - lest you persist in denying Muhammad, may Allah bless him and grant him peace, and what happened to the nations before you befall you.

The wrongdoers' cries of despair continue until they are completely destroyed, serving as a cautionary tale for others.

Verse 16

وَمَا خَلَقْنَا السَّمَاءَ وَالْأَرْضَ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَا لَاعِبِينَ

We did not create the heavens and the earth and everything in between for sport.

And We did not create the heavens and the earth and what is between them in vain and without purpose, but rather to establish proof upon you - O people - and that you may take lesson from all of that, so you may know that He who created that, nothing resembles Him, and worship is not befitting except for Him.

Allah states that the creation of the heavens and the earth was not without purpose, but rather to establish proof of His existence and power.

Verse 17

لَوْ أَرَدْنَا أَنْ نَتَّخِذَ لَهْوًا لَاتَّخَذْنَاهُ مِنْ لَدُنَّا إِنْ كُنَّا فَاعِلِينَ

Had We intended to take ˹some˺ amusement, We could have found it in Our presence, if that had been Our Will.

If We had desired to take a diversion from offspring or a companion, We would have taken it from Ourselves, not from you. We would not have done that, for it is impossible for Us to have an offspring or a companion.

Allah clarifies that if He desired amusement, He would not have taken it from His creation, affirming that He is above such things.

Verse 18

بَلْ نَقْذِفُ بِالْحَقِّ عَلَى الْبَاطِلِ فَيَدْمَغُهُ فَإِذَا هُوَ زَاهِقٌ ۚ وَلَكُمُ الْوَيْلُ مِمَّا تَصِفُونَ

In fact, We hurl the truth against falsehood, leaving it crushed, and it quickly vanishes. And woe be to you for what you claim!

Rather, We cast the truth, and We make it clear, and it overwhelms falsehood, and then it is gone and diminished. And for you is the punishment in the Hereafter - O polytheists - for describing your Lord with other than His befitting attributes.

Allah clarifies that truth is cast against falsehood and destroys it, and warns those who associate partners with God of punishment.

Verse 19

وَلَهُ مَنْ فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ ۚ وَمَنْ عِنْدَهُ لَا يَسْتَكْبِرُونَ عَنْ عِبَادَتِهِ وَلَا يَسْتَحْسِرُونَ

To Him belong all those in the heavens and the earth. And those nearest to Him are not too proud to worship Him, nor do they tire.

And to Allah, glory be to Him, belongs everyone in the heavens and the earth. And those who are with Him, from the angels, do not disdain His worship, nor do they tire of it. So how can it be permissible to associate with Him what is His servant and creation?

This verse emphasizes that all in the heavens and earth belong to Allah and that even the angels do not tire of worshipping Him, and this contrasts with the wrongdoers who reject Him.

Verse 20

يُسَبِّحُونَ اللَّيْلَ وَالنَّهَارَ لَا يَفْتُرُونَ

They glorify ˹Him˺ day and night, never wavering.

They remember Allah and declare His purity always, they do not weaken nor do they tire.

Angels glorify Allah continuously, day and night without ever tiring, which shows that those who turn away from Allah are in clear error.

Verse 21

أَمِ اتَّخَذُوا آلِهَةً مِنَ الْأَرْضِ هُمْ يُنْشِرُونَ

Or have they taken gods from the earth, who can raise the dead?

How is it correct for the polytheists to take incapable gods from the earth that are unable to bring the dead to life?

The verse questions how people could worship beings on Earth that cannot create nor give life, highlighting the absurdity of idolatry.

Verse 22

لَوْ كَانَ فِيهِمَا آلِهَةٌ إِلَّا اللَّهُ لَفَسَدَتَا ۚ فَسُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَرْشِ عَمَّا يَصِفُونَ

Had there been other gods besides Allah in the heavens or the earth, both ˹realms˺ would have surely been corrupted. So Glorified is Allah, Lord of the Throne, far above what they claim.

If there were in the heavens and the earth gods other than Allah, glory be to Him, managing their affairs, then their system would be disrupted. So exalted be Allah, Lord of the Throne, and sanctified from what the deniers and disbelievers describe, of falsehood, fabrication, and every deficiency.

If there were multiple gods, the heavens and earth would be in chaos, proving that there is only one true God, Allah.

Verse 23

لَا يُسْأَلُ عَمَّا يَفْعَلُ وَهُمْ يُسْأَلُونَ

He cannot be questioned about what He does, but they will ˹all˺ be questioned.

Indeed, from the indications of His uniqueness, glory be to Him, in creation and worship, is that He is not questioned about His decree in His creation, and all of His creation are questioned about their deeds.

Allah cannot be questioned about His actions, while all of His creation will be held accountable for their deeds, highlighting the difference in power and accountability.

Verse 24

أَمِ اتَّخَذُوا مِنْ دُونِهِ آلِهَةً ۖ قُلْ هَاتُوا بُرْهَانَكُمْ ۖ هَٰذَا ذِكْرُ مَنْ مَعِيَ وَذِكْرُ مَنْ قَبْلِي ۗ بَلْ أَكْثَرُهُمْ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ الْحَقَّ ۖ فَهُمْ مُعْرِضُونَ

Or have they taken other gods besides Him? Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “Show ˹me˺ your proof. Here is ˹the Quran,˺ the Reminder for those with me; along with ˹earlier Scriptures,˺ the Reminder for those before me.” But most of them do not know the truth, so they turn away.

Have these polytheists taken gods other than Allah that benefit and harm, give life and cause death? Say - O Messenger - to them: "Bring what you have of proof for what you have taken as gods." For there is no evidence in the Quran which I came with nor in the previous books for what you have gone to, and they have not associated except out of ignorance and imitation, so they are turning away from the truth and denying it.

This verse challenges the polytheists to bring proof for their idols and emphasizes that both the Quran and previous scriptures do not support their claims, showing that the association with others was based only on ignorance.

Verse 25

وَمَا أَرْسَلْنَا مِنْ قَبْلِكَ مِنْ رَسُولٍ إِلَّا نُوحِي إِلَيْهِ أَنَّهُ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا أَنَا فَاعْبُدُونِ

We never sent a messenger before you ˹O Prophet˺ without revealing to him: “There is no god ˹worthy of worship˺ except Me, so worship Me ˹alone˺.”

And We did not send before you, [O Prophet], any messenger except that We revealed to him that, "There is no deity worthy of worship except Allah , so devote yourselves to Him alone."

This verse emphasizes that all prophets were sent with the same core message: worship Allah alone, as He is the only true God.

Verse 26

وَقَالُوا اتَّخَذَ الرَّحْمَٰنُ وَلَدًا ۗ سُبْحَانَهُ ۚ بَلْ عِبَادٌ مُكْرَمُونَ

And they say, “The Most Compassionate has offspring!” Glory be to Him! In fact, those ˹angels˺ are only ˹His˺ honoured servants,

And the polytheists said, "The Most Merciful has taken a son," claiming that the angels are daughters of Allah. Exalted is Allah above that! Rather, the angels are honored servants of Allah, distinguished by virtues, and in their good obedience, they do not speak except by what their Lord commands them, nor do they do anything until He gives them permission.

This verse refutes the claim that Allah has offspring, clarifying that angels are honored servants of Allah, not His children.

Verse 27

لَا يَسْبِقُونَهُ بِالْقَوْلِ وَهُمْ بِأَمْرِهِ يَعْمَلُونَ

who do not speak until He has spoken, ˹only˺ acting at His command.

And the polytheists said, "The Most Merciful has taken a son," claiming that the angels are daughters of Allah. Exalted is Allah above that! Rather, the angels are honored servants of Allah, distinguished by virtues, and in their good obedience, they do not speak except by what their Lord commands them, nor do they do anything until He gives them permission.

Angels are obedient to Allah, acting only upon His commands and not speaking without His permission, reinforcing their status as subservient beings.

Verse 28

يَعْلَمُ مَا بَيْنَ أَيْدِيهِمْ وَمَا خَلْفَهُمْ وَلَا يَشْفَعُونَ إِلَّا لِمَنِ ارْتَضَىٰ وَهُمْ مِنْ خَشْيَتِهِ مُشْفِقُونَ

He ˹fully˺ knows what is ahead of them and what is behind them. They do not intercede except for whom He approves, and they tremble in awe of Him.

And there is no deed of the angels, whether past or future, except that Allah Almighty knows it, and He records it for them. And they do not intercede except for those whom Allah approves of their intercession for him, and they are, out of fear of Allah, wary of disobeying His command and prohibition.

Allah knows all actions of the angels. They can only intercede on behalf of those whom He permits, and they are in awe of Him.

Verse 29

وَمَنْ يَقُلْ مِنْهُمْ إِنِّي إِلَٰهٌ مِنْ دُونِهِ فَذَٰلِكَ نَجْزِيهِ جَهَنَّمَ ۚ كَذَٰلِكَ نَجْزِي الظَّالِمِينَ

Whoever of them were to say, “I am a god besides Him,” they would be rewarded with Hell by Us. This is how We reward the wrongdoers.

And whoever among the angels claims that he is a god with Allah - hypothetically - then his recompense will be Hell. Like that recompense We will recompense every unjust polytheist.

If any angel were to claim divinity, they would be punished with Hell, like all wrongdoers, reinforcing the message that there is only one God.

Verse 30

أَوَلَمْ يَرَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا أَنَّ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ كَانَتَا رَتْقًا فَفَتَقْنَاهُمَا ۖ وَجَعَلْنَا مِنَ الْمَاءِ كُلَّ شَيْءٍ حَيٍّ ۖ أَفَلَا يُؤْمِنُونَ

Do the disbelievers not realize that the heavens and earth were ˹once˺ one mass then We split them apart? And We created from water every living thing. Will they not then believe?

Have those who disbelieved not considered that the heavens and the earth were a joined entity, so We separated them, and We sent down rain from the sky and brought forth vegetation from the earth and We made from water every living thing? So will they not believe, so that they may acknowledge what they witness and single out Allah for worship?

This verse discusses the creation of the universe, noting that the heavens and earth were once joined and then separated, and that all life originates from water as evidence of Allah's power and Oneness.

Verse 31

وَجَعَلْنَا فِي الْأَرْضِ رَوَاسِيَ أَنْ تَمِيدَ بِهِمْ وَجَعَلْنَا فِيهَا فِجَاجًا سُبُلًا لَعَلَّهُمْ يَهْتَدُونَ

And We have placed firm mountains upon the earth so it does not shake with them, and made in it broad pathways so they may find their way.

And We placed within the earth mountains to stabilize it, so that it would not shake, and We placed in it broad paths, in the hope that creation might be guided to their livelihoods and to the Oneness of their Creator.

Mountains serve as anchors to stabilize the earth, and pathways were created for people to travel and find their way, highlighting Allah's provision and guidance.

Verse 32

وَجَعَلْنَا السَّمَاءَ سَقْفًا مَحْفُوظًا ۖ وَهُمْ عَنْ آيَاتِهَا مُعْرِضُونَ

And We have made the sky a well-protected canopy, still they turn away from its signs.

And We made the sky a protected ceiling for the earth, without pillars to hold it up. And it is guarded and cannot fall, nor can the devils penetrate it, and the disbelievers are heedless and neglectful of reflecting on the signs of the sky (the sun, moon, and stars).

The sky acts as a protected canopy, and despite the clear signs in the heavens, disbelievers ignore these signs of Allah's creation.

Verse 33

وَهُوَ الَّذِي خَلَقَ اللَّيْلَ وَالنَّهَارَ وَالشَّمْسَ وَالْقَمَرَ ۖ كُلٌّ فِي فَلَكٍ يَسْبَحُونَ

And He is the One Who created the day and the night, the sun and the moon—each travelling in an orbit.

And Allah is the One who created the night for people to rest in, and the day for them to seek their livelihoods. And He created the sun as a sign for the day and the moon as a sign for the night, and each of them has an orbit in which it travels and floats, and from which it does not deviate.

Allah created the day and night, the sun and the moon, each traveling in its own orbit, demonstrating His meticulous design of the universe.

Verse 34

وَمَا جَعَلْنَا لِبَشَرٍ مِنْ قَبْلِكَ الْخُلْدَ ۖ أَفَإِنْ مِتَّ فَهُمُ الْخَالِدُونَ

We have not granted immortality to any human before you ˹O Prophet˺: so if you die, will they live forever?

And We did not grant to any human before you, [O Messenger], permanence in this world. So, if you die, will they then hope for immortality after you? That will not be. And in this verse is evidence that Al-Khidr, peace be upon him, has died, for he was a human.

This verse emphasizes that no human is granted immortality, including the prophets, and reminds people that death is inevitable.

Verse 35

كُلُّ نَفْسٍ ذَائِقَةُ الْمَوْتِ ۗ وَنَبْلُوكُمْ بِالشَّرِّ وَالْخَيْرِ فِتْنَةً ۖ وَإِلَيْنَا تُرْجَعُونَ

Every soul will taste death. And We test you ˹O humanity˺ with good and evil as a trial, then to Us you will ˹all˺ be returned.
