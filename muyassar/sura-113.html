Sura 113

Verse 1

قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ الْفَلَقِ

Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “I seek refuge in the Lord of the daybreak

Say, [O Muhammad], "I seek refuge with the Lord of the daybreak, which is the morning."

This verse begins a prayer seeking refuge in the Lord of the daybreak from all evil.

Verse 2

مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ

from the evil of whatever He has created,

From the evil of all created things and their harm.

This verse seeks protection from the evil of all created things.

Verse 3

وَمِنْ شَرِّ غَاسِقٍ إِذَا وَقَبَ

and from the evil of the night when it grows dark,

And from the evil of a dark night when it enters and pervades, and what it contains of evils and harm.

This verse seeks protection from the evil of the dark night.

Verse 4

وَمِنْ شَرِّ النَّفَّاثَاتِ فِي الْعُقَدِ

and from the evil of those ˹witches casting spells by˺ blowing onto knots,

And from the evil of the witches who blow on knots for the purpose of sorcery.

This verse seeks refuge from the evil of those who practice witchcraft and sorcery.

Verse 5

وَمِنْ شَرِّ حَاسِدٍ إِذَا حَسَدَ

and from the evil of an envier when they envy.”

And from the evil of an envious hater when he envies people for what Allah has granted them of blessings, and desires that they be removed from them, and harm befalls them.

This verse seeks refuge from the harm of the envious when they envy others.
