Sura 093

Verse 1

وَالضُّحَىٰ

By the morning sunlight,

Allah swears by the time of forenoon, which is meant to be the whole day, and by the night when it becomes still with creation and its darkness intensifies. And Allah swears by what He wills of His creations, but it is not permissible for a created being to swear by other than his Creator, for swearing by other than Allah is polytheism. Your Lord has not forsaken you, O Prophet, nor has He hated you for the delay of revelation to you.

Allah swears by the morning sunlight, symbolizing the day, and the night when it becomes still.

Verse 4

وَلَلْآخِرَةُ خَيْرٌ لَكَ مِنَ الْأُولَىٰ

And the next life is certainly far better for you than this one.

And the Hereafter is better for you than this worldly life, and your Lord, O Prophet, will surely give you all kinds of blessings in the Hereafter, so that you will be pleased with that.

The Hereafter is far better for the Prophet than this worldly life.

Allah promises the Prophet blessings in the Hereafter that will completely satisfy him.

Verse 6

أَلَمْ يَجِدْكَ يَتِيمًا فَآوَىٰ

Did He not find you as an orphan then sheltered you?

Did He not find you an orphan before, then sheltered and protected you? And He found you not knowing what the Book or faith was, so He taught you what you did not know, and enabled you to do the best of deeds? And He found you poor, then He provided you with your sustenance, and made your soul content with contentment and patience?

Allah reminds the Prophet of His favor, that He found him as an orphan and sheltered him.

Verse 9

فَأَمَّا الْيَتِيمَ فَلَا تَقْهَرْ

So do not oppress the orphan,

So as for the orphan, do not mistreat him, and as for the beggar, do not repel him, but feed him, fulfill his need, and as for the favor of your Lord that He has bestowed upon you, then speak about it.

The Prophet is instructed not to oppress the orphan.
